Question 571 :
Which one of the following States shares boundaries with the maximum number of other States of India ? [CPO AC 2003]

  1. Madhya Pradesh
  2. Maharashtra
  3. Assam
  4. Bihar
   Assam

Question 572 :
Which foreign country is closest to Andaman Islands? [CPO SI 2003]

  1. Sri Lanka
  2. Myanmar
  3. Indonesia
  4. Pakistan
   Myanmar

Question 573 :
Which of the following is not a plantation crop?

  1. Coffee
  2. Rubber
  3. Sugarcane
  4. Tea
   Sugarcane

Question 574 :
The premonsoon mango showers occur predominantly in

  1. West Bengal and Assam
  2. Deccan Plateau
  3. Gujarat and Maharashtra
  4. Kerala and Karnataka
   West Bengal and Assam

Question 575 :
Which of the following tribes is not found in Central India ? [IAS 2003]

  1. Gonds
  2. Todas
  3. Bhils
  4. Munda
   Todas

Question 576 :
Which European country has over 200 volcanoes, many of them active?

  1. Norway
  2. Finland
  3. Ireland
  4. Iceland
   Iceland

Question 577 :
Which of the following soils is very hard to cultivate? [Railways 1994]

  1. Alluvial
  2. Black
  3. Red
  4. Sandy
   Sandy

Question 578 :
Which of the following mountain passes lies outside India ?

  1. Khyber
  2. Bomdila
  3. Bara Lacha La
  4. Shipkila
   Khyber

Question 579 :
Consider the following statements regarding Andaman and Nicobar Islands: 1. It enjoys equatorial climate 2. This is the only place in India where a volcano is located 3. This is the only place in India where coral bed is found . - Which of the statements given above is/are correct? [NDA 2010]

  1. 1, 2 and 3
  2. 1 and 2
  3. 2 and 3
  4. 1 only
   1 and 2

Question 580 :
People or north Africa are generally

  1. Negroid
  2. Hematic
  3. Mongoloid
  4. None of these
   Hematic

Question 581 :
Which of the following characteristics are associated with the dry monsoon forest of India? 1. Annual rainfall is below 50 cm 2. The trees are short rooted 3. Thorny shrubs and grassland 4. Mango, Mahua, Sisam and Keeker are the prominent trees.

  1. 1 and 2
  2. 2 and 4
  3. 3 and 4
  4. 1, 2 and 3
   3 and 4

Question 582 :
The length of India's coastline is about: [SBI 2013]

  1. 5900 km
  2. 6100 km
  3. 7000 km
  4. 7500 km
   7500 km

Question 583 :
Duncan Passage is situated between: [CDS 1999]

  1. Minicoy and Amindiv
  2. Minicoy and Maldives
  3. Little Andaman and Car Nicobar
  4. South Andaman and Little Andaman
   South Andaman and Little Andaman

Question 584 :
Which states of India have a common border with Pakistan? 1. Jammu and Kashmir 2. Himachal Pradesh 3. Punjab 4. Gujarat 5. Rajasthan

  1. I, II, III, V
  2. I, II, III, IV
  3. I, III, IV
  4. I, III, IV, V
   I, III, IV, V

Question 585 :
The highest plateau in the world is

  1. Plateau of Tibet
  2. Deccan Plateau
  3. Plateau of Iran
  4. Spanish Plateau
   Plateau of Tibet

Question 586 :
Among the following States, which one has the largest forest area? [IAS 2008]

  1. Gujarat
  2. Karnataka
  3. Orissa
  4. Tamil Nadu
   Gujarat

Question 587 :
The Earth's axis is tilted at an angle of ____ degrees from the perpendicular to its orbital plane.

  1. 17.5°
  2. 23.5°
  3. 36°
  4. 45°
   23.5°

Question 588 :
The Himalayas are formed of parallel fold ranges of which the oldest range is : [IAS 1994]

  1. the Siwalik Range
  2. the Lesser Himalayas
  3. the Great Himalayan Range
  4. the Dhaula Dhar Range
   the Great Himalayan Range

Question 589 :
In which State is the Guru Shikhar Peak located? [IAS 2007]

  1. Rajasthan
  2. Gujarat
  3. Madhya Pradesh
  4. Maharashtra
   Rajasthan

Question 590 :
The capital of Lakshadweep is : [Stenographers' Exam 1990]

  1. Port Blair
  2. Silvassa
  3. Aizawl
  4. Kavaratti
   Kavaratti

Question 591 :
Which one of the following does NOT lie in Maharashtra? [UP PCS 2011]

  1. Balaghat Range
  2. Harishchandra Range
  3. Mandav Hills
  4. Satmala Hills
   Mandav Hills

Question 592 :
Which of the following States has the largest number of people belonging to Scheduled Caste? [BPSC 2011]

  1. Bihar
  2. West Bengal
  3. Uttar Pradesh
  4. Punjab
   Punjab

Question 593 :
Which one of the following statements is wrong regarding the Vale of Kashmir ? [CDS 2002]

  1. It is a synclinal valley
  2. It is situated 'between the Pir Panjal Range and the Lesser Himalayas
  3. It is drained by the river Jhelum
  4. Previously it was a bed of a great lake, subsequently uplifted.
   It is situated 'between the Pir Panjal Range and the Lesser Himalayas

Question 594 :
The Konkan Coast stretches between:

  1. Goa and Cochin
  2. Goa and Mumbai
  3. Goa and Daman
  4. Goa and Diu
   Goa and Cochin

Question 595 :
Which of the following towns is located at easternmost? [IAS 2003]

  1. Bokaro
  2. Jamshedpur
  3. Patna
  4. Ranchi
   Jamshedpur

Question 596 :
The wettest place in the world is Cherrapunji (Assam). The Hottest place is

  1. Azizia (Libya)
  2. Ramagundam (India)
  3. Stwart Island (New Zealand)
  4. Kandy (Sri Lanka)
   Azizia (Libya)

Question 597 :
The Rajasthan Canal Project coven

  1. Punjab
  2. Punjab, Haryana and Rajasthan
  3. Haryana
  4. Rajasthan
   Punjab, Haryana and Rajasthan

Question 598 :
Which one of the following is most prone to earthquakes? [CDS 2000]

  1. Coastal plains
  2. Old shields
  3. Plateaus
  4. Young folded mountains
   Young folded mountains

Question 599 :
What separates Daman and Diu? [RRB 1993]

  1. Narmada River
  2. Gulf of Khambat
  3. Gir Hills
  4. Tapti River
   Gulf of Khambat

Question 600 :
__________ forms the western boundary of the Indian sub-continent.

  1. Aravalli
  2. Pirpanjal
  3. Karakoram
