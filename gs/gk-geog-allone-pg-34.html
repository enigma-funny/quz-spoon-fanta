Question 991 :
A heavenly body which takes nearly the same time to complete one rotation and one revolution is

  1. an asteroid
  2. a comet
  3. Moon
  4. Mars
   Moon

Question 992 :
Critical criteria for the location of an aluminium plant in India are the availability of bauxite and: [CDS 1999]

  1. coal
  2. electricity
  3. labour
  4. ready market
   electricity

Question 993 :
Which one among the following is a correct sequence of production of coal in the Indian States in descending order ? [NDA 2011]

  1. Jharkhand - Madhya Pradesh - West Bengal - Meghalaya
  2. West Bengal - Madhya Pradesh - Jharkhand - Meghalaya
  3. Jharkhand - West Bengal - Meghalaya - Madhya Pradesh
  4. Madhya Pradesh - Jharkhand - West Bengal – Meghalaya
   Jharkhand - Madhya Pradesh - West Bengal - Meghalaya

Question 997 :

Question 998 :

Question 999 :
The primary purpose of constructing the Farakka barrage is to: [CDS 1999]

  1. preserve and maintain navigation
  2. supply drinking water
  3. meet the chronic power shortage
  4. bring additional areas into irrigation
   preserve and maintain navigation

Question 1000 :
Which of the following is/are wrong? 1. Bhadravati steel plant - not located near coal deposits 2. Bokaro and Bhilai steel plants - German collaboration 3. Durgapur steel plant - British help

  1. 1 and 2
  2. 2, 3 and 4
  3. 1, 2 and 3
  4. 2 and 3
   2 and 3

Question 1001 :
The three major cereals in India under high yielding varieties in declining order are:

  1. wheat, paddy and coarse cereals
  2. barley, wheat and coarse cereals
  3. paddy, wheat and coarse cereals
  4. coarse cereals, paddy and wheat

Question 1004 :
The leading states in the production of cotton in India are: [NDA 2003]

  1. Maharashtra and Gujarat
  2. Gujarat and Andhra Pradesh
  3. Maharashtra and Punjab
  4. Gujarat and Punjab

Question 1005 :

Question 1006 :

Question 1007 :
Gujarat is noted for the cultivation of

  1. Cotton
  2. Sugarcane

  4. Jute

Question 1008 :
The actual amount or moisture contained in the air is known as

  1. relative humidity
  2. moisture content
  3. dew point
  4. absolute humidity
   absolute humidity

Question 1009 :
Which one of the following regions experiences three cropping season for rice in India (Rice Bowl of India) ? [IAS 2003]

  1. Krishna-Godavari delta
  2. Brahmaputra valley
  3. Coastal Kerala
  4. Littoral Tamil Nadu
   Brahmaputra valley

Question 1010 :
Consider the following statements about anticyclones: (1) The winds are divergent, and spiral out from the centre of high pressure (2) The air moves clockwise in the Northern Hemisphere and anticlockwise in Southern Hemisphere (3) It is accompanied by heavy rainfall (4) A sudden rise in barometric reading heralds the approach of anticyclone Which of these statements are correct?

  1. 1 and 2
  2. 1 and 4
  3. 1, 2 and 3
  4. 1, 2 and 4
   1, 2 and 4

Question 1011 :
The following are the major oilseeds produced in India: [IAS 1997] 1. Sesamum 2. Mustard 3. Soyabean - Which one of the following is the correct sequence of the descending order of the quantity of their production?

  1. 1, 2, 3, 4
  2. 3, 2, 4, 1
  3. 2, 4, 3, 1
  4. 3, 4, 2, 1
   3, 2, 4, 1

Question 1012 :
Which one of the following types of plains is not formed by the action of the riven?

  1. Loess plains
  2. Alluvial plains
  3. Flood plain deposits
  4. Deltas

Question 1016 :
Salinization occurs when the irrigation water accumulated in the soil evaporates, leaving behind salts and minerals. What are the effects of salinization on the irrigated land? [CSAT 2011]

  1. It greatly increases the crop production
  2. It makes some soils impermeable
  3. It raises the water table
  4. It fills the air spaces in the soil with water
   It makes some soils impermeable
