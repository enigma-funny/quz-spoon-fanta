Question 211 :
Which or the following Indian islands lies between India and Sri Lanka?

  1. Elephanta
  2. Rameshwaram
  3. Nicobar
  4. Salsette
   Nicobar

Question 212 :
Taking into account the amount of rainfall occurring from June to September, which one of the following is the correct order of cities in terms of rainfall? [IAS 2007]

  1. Kolkata > Ahmedabad > Allahabad
  2. Kolkata > Allahabad > Ahmedabad
  3. Allahabad > Kolkata > Ahmedabad
  4. Ahmedabad > Kolkata > Allahabad
   Kolkata > Allahabad > Ahmedabad

  1. The expansion of poorly planned, low-density, auto-dependent development, which spreads out over large amounts of land                          
  2. A continuous urban spread constituting a town and its adjoining outgrowths, or two or more physically contiguous towns together with or without outgrowths of such towns

Question 238 :

  2. 3 and 4
  3. 2, 3 and 4
  4. 1, 2, 3 and 4

  3. Both 1 and 2
