Question 91 :
Coal, which is almost pure carbons which hardly has any Impurities, which is smokeless and which finds use in the steel industry is called

  1. coking coal
  2. anthracite
  3. bituminous coal
  4. lignite
   coking coal

Question 92 :
Moho discontinuity refers to a discontinuity between the _____ and _____ layers of the earth.

  1. Sial and Sima
  2. Sima and Mantle
  3. Sial and Mantle
  4. Mantle and Core
   Sima and Mantle

Question 93 :
The country worst affected by cyclones and accompanying floods is ______.

  1. Indonesia
  2. Sri Lanka
  3. Pakistan
  4. Bangladesh
   Bangladesh

Question 94 :
The wettest place in the world is Cherrapunji (Assam). The Hottest place is

  1. Azizia (Libya)
  2. Ramagundam (India)
  3. Stwart Island (New Zealand)
  4. Kandy (Sri Lanka)
   Azizia (Libya)

Question 95 :
Which or the following is not a proof of Earth being an oblate spheroid?

  1. When a ship goes out to sea, first the hull disappears then the upper parts
  2. The shadow of the Earth on the Sun during solar eclipse is always curved
  3. Photographs of the Earth taken by artificial satellites have confirmed the fact
  4. it has been found that polar circumference is lesser than equatorial circumference
   The shadow of the Earth on the Sun during solar eclipse is always curved

Question 98 :
For short-term climatic perdictions, which one of the following events is associated with occasional weak monsoon rains in the Indian sub-continent?

  1. La Nina
  2. Movement of Jet Streams
  3. El Nino and Southern Oscillations

Question 99 :

Question 107 :
Which among the following statements about the earth is not true?

  1. The Earth makes one complete revolution round the Sun every 365 days, 5 hours, 48 minutes, and 46 seconds
  2. The Earth makes one complete revolution on its axis in 48 hours
  3. The Earth revolves in its orbit round the Sun at a speed of 66,000 miles per hour
  4. The Earth rotates on its axis at an equatorial speed of more than 1000 miles per hour

Question 113 :
The difference in mean temperature between the hottest and coldest months in a year is known as

Question 114 :
The population of India has of late been growing very fast because

  1. not enough attention has been paid to methods of population control
  2. the death rate has been dropping on account of better medical and health facilities
  3. the birth rate has continuously been increasing
  4. industrialization has provided a fillip to growth of population
