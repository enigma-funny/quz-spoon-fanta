Question 91 :
Consider the following statements: 1. Cherrapunji is located on the south side of Garo hills. 2. Cherrapunji is about 1700 m above mean sea level. - Which of the statements given above is/are correct? [IAS 2008]

  1. 1 only
  2. 2 only
  3. Both 1 and 2
  4. Neither 1 nor 2
   Both 1 and 2

Question 102 :
Which one of the following statements is not correct? [IAS 2005]

  1. The Western Ghats are relatively higher in their northern region

  3. Tapi river lies to the south of Satpura

Question 115 :
Why is that India has one of the lowest agricultural yields in the world?
