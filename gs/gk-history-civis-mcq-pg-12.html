Question 331 :
Which of the following is correct regarding the Governor of a State?

  1. He can recommend to the President to impose President's Rule in the State.
  2. No money bill can be introduced in the State Legislature without his prior permission.
  3. He has the power of issuing ordinances when the legislature is not in session.
  4. All of the above
  All of the above

Question 332 :
Who proposed the Preamble before the Drafting Committee of the Constitution?

  1. Jawahar Lal Nehru
  2. B.R. Ambedkar
  3. B.N. Rau

Question 337 :
Which one of the following feature was not borrowed by the Indian Constitution from the British Constitution?

  1. Parliamentary system of Government.
  2. Rule of Law.
  3. Election provisions.
  4. None of the above.

Question 338 :
Under which of the following the 'Untouchability' has been declared abolished in India?

  1. Protection of Civil Rights Act.
  2. Schedule Castes and Schedule Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act, 1989.
  3. Constitution of India.
  4. By all the above three

Question 342 :
Under the Constitution of India, which one of the following is NOT a fundamental duty?

  1. To vote in public elections
  2. To develop the scientific temper
  3. To safeguard public property
  4. To abide by the Constitution and respect its deals

Question 343 :

Question 346 :

Question 350 :
The President is elected for a period of five years and his term of office is calculated from the

  1. Last day of the month he assumes charge
  2. Date of his election result
  3. Day he assumes charge
  4. First day of the month he assumes charge

Question 351 :
Whether judiciary is the part of the `other authority' in Art. 12 of the Constitution?

  1. Yes.
  2. No.
  3. Both depend upon circumstances.
  4. None of the above.

Question 352 :
Which one of the following has the power to initiate the motion of impeachment of the President of India?

  1. Rajya Sabha
  2. Lok Sabha
  3. Speaker of the Lok Sabha
  4. Both the houses of the parliament

Question 354 :

Question 355 :

  4. Article 167

Question 357 :
The word `Sovereign' in the Preamble of the Constitution of India emphasizes that India is:

  1. A democratic and republic country.
  2. No more dependent upon any outside authority.
  3. Internally empowered to rule within the State territory.
  4. Not bound by external commands and the Government of India is equipped with the power to rule within India.
  Not bound by external commands and the Government of India is equipped with the power to rule within India.
