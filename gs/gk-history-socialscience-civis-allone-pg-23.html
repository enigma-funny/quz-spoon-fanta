Question 661 :
During the later half of the nineteenth century, the Indian intelligentsia started protesting against the policies of the colonial rule by various means. Which one of the following was not a part of that protest?

  1. 1, 3, 2, 4
  2. 2, 1, 4, 3
  3. 3, 4, 1, 2
  4. 3, 1, 4, 2

Question 662 :
The Gupta gold and silver issues were initially based on the coins of the —

  1. Ochre coloured pottery
  2. Black and red ware

Question 681 :
The East India Company began to use the term ‘Investments in India’ for—

  1. Their bullion imports to purchase Indian goods
  2. The loot from Buxar for purchasing Indian goods
  3. The profits made from duty free inland trade and the surplus from Diwani revenue
  4. The borrowed money from Indian money lenders for purchases in India money
