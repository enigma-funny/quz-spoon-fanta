Question 421 :
Who among the following is said to be the founder of the pasupata cult?

  1. Digambara jainas
  2. Kapalikas
  3. Buddhist Tantriks
  4. Bhagavatas
   Kapalikas

Question 422 :
What was the name of the Gurjara-Pratihara king who was defeated by the Pala king Devapala?

  1. Mihirbhoja
  2. Nagabhatta II
  3. Mahipala
  4. Mahendrapala

Question 423 :
The Post-Gupta social structure in North India was marked by the —

  1. Growing importance of women

Question 428 :
Subhash Chand Bose was elected President of the Indian National Congress in —

  1. Right speech, right action, right exertion and right mindness
  2. Right meditation, right resolution, right view and right means of livelihood
  3. Dukham (Misery), Samudaya (Thirst, attachment etc.).
