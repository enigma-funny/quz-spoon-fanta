Question 1561 :
To which kind of rock does marble belong?

  1. Metamorphic rock
  2. Sedimentary rock
  3. Igneous rock
  4. Volcanic rock
   Metamorphic rock

Question 1562 :
What is Gomia in Jharkhand famous for?

  1. Coal fields
  2. Manganese mines
  3. Fertilizer plant
  4. Explosives factory
   Explosives factory

Question 1563 :
Approximately what percentage of world's cattle population is to be found in India?

  1. 50%
  2. 18%
  3. 80%
  4. 7%
   18%

Question 1564 :
Where is Arvi earth station for satellite communication?

  1. Uttar Pradesh
  2. Tamil Nadu
  3. Maharashtra
  4. Gujarat
   Gujarat

Question 1565 :
What is the most important geographic use of the Himalayas to India?

  1. Prevention of invasions
  2. Valuable source of timber
  3. They prevent the monsoons from crossing to the north and also serve as a reservoir of water from which our perennial rivers flow
  4. They protect India from the icy cold north winds
   They prevent the monsoons from crossing to the north and also serve as a reservoir of water from which our perennial rivers flow

Question 1566 :
The first fertilizer plant was set up at

  1. Nangal (Punjab)
  2. Sindri (Bihar)
  3. Alwaye (Kerala)
  4. Trombay (Maharashtra)
   Sindri (Bihar)

Question 1567 :
The chief coal bearing formation in India is known as the

  1. Siwaliks
  2. Gondwanas
  3. Vindhyans
  4. Cuddappahs
   Gondwanas

Question 1568 :
October Heat' is caused due to

  1. the absence of rain
  2. combination of high temperature and excessive humidity
  3. dry hot weather
  4. None of the above
   combination of high temperature and excessive humidity

Question 1569 :
Which of the following is not a tributary of the Krishna?

  1. Tungabhadra
  2. Malaprabha
  3. Ghataprabha
  4. Amravati
   Amravati

Question 1570 :
The roadways linking Mumbai and Agra passes through

  1. Nasik and Gwalior
  2. Pune and Bhopal
  3. Badodara and Jhansi
  4. Jaipur and Kanpur
   Nasik and Gwalior

Question 1571 :
Periyar Wild Life Sanctuary is located in ________ State.

  1. Andhra Pradesh
  2. Tamil Nadu
  3. Kerala
  4. Karnataka
   Kerala

Question 1572 :
Which of the following pairs of states and their important crops are correctly matched? 1. Kerala Tapioca 2. Maharashtra Cotton 3. West Bengal Jute 4. Gujarat Groundnut. - Select the correct answer by using the des given below: Codes:

  1. 1, 2 and 3
  2. 1, 2 and 4
  3. 1, 3 and 4
  4. 2, 3 and 4
   2, 3 and 4

Question 1573 :
Which of the following rivers of India carries the most water

  1. Ganga
  2. Brahmaputra
  3. Yamuna
  4. Godavari
   Brahmaputra

Question 1574 :
The territorial waters of India extends up to

  1. 5 nautical miles
  2. 12 nautical miles
  3. 15 nautical miles
  4. 2 nautical miles
   12 nautical miles

Question 1575 :
India lies

  1. mainly in the northern hemisphere
  2. entirely in the northern hemisphere
  3. on the Equator
  4. more in the southern hemisphere
   entirely in the northern hemisphere

Question 1576 :
Which of the following places are known for paper manufacturing industry? 1. Yamunanagu2. Guwahati3. Shahabad4. Ballarpur. Choose the correct answer using the codes given below:

  1. 1, 2 and 3
  2. 1, 2 and 4
  3. 1, 3 and 4
  4. 2, 3 and 4
   1, 2 and 4

Question 1577 :
The moat important commercial forests of India are

  1. Tropical evergreen
  2. Mangrove
  3. Tropical deciduous
  4. Coniferous
   Tropical deciduous

Question 1578 :
The clouds extending from one side of horizon to other as 3 dark grey sheet having uniform base are called: [IAS 2004]

  1. Stratus clouds
  2. Cumulus clouds
  3. Cirrus clouds
  4. Nimbus clouds
   Stratus clouds

Question 1579 :
Soyabeans are rich in

  1. Vitamin A
  2. Proteins
  3. Minerals
  4. Carbohydrates
   Proteins

Question 1580 :
The second largest east-flowing Peninsular river is

  1. Godavari
  2. Krishna
  3. Cauvery
  4. Mahanadi
   Krishna

Question 1581 :
Wind erosion is common in

  1. desert
  2. cold areas
  3. areas where there is thick vegetation
  4. areas of heavy rainfall
   desert

Question 1582 :
Black soil derived from volcanic rocks with humus is suitable for growing

  1. rice
  2. wheat
  3. cotton
  4. grapes
   cotton

Question 1583 :
Which of the following is not a characteristic of Peninsular rivers?

  1. Seasonal flow
  2. Meandering tendency often shifting their beds
  3. Flow through shallow valleys
  4. Little erosional activity
   Meandering tendency often shifting their beds

Question 1584 :
The four important ports of the West Coast of India, from South to North, in that order, are

  1. Kandla, Mumbai, Goa, Cochin
  2. Goa, Mumbai, Cochin, Kandla
  3. Mumbai, Kandla, Goa, Cochin
  4. Cochin, Goa, Mumbai, Kandla
   Cochin, Goa, Mumbai, Kandla

Question 1585 :
The plateau rich in minerals in India is

  1. Malwa Plateau
  2. Chhotanagpur Plateau
  3. Deccan Plateau
  4. Mysuru Plateau
   Chhotanagpur Plateau

Question 1586 :
Prince, Victoria and Alexandria are the three important and busy docks at

  1. Cochin
  2. Visakhapatnam
  3. Chennai
  4. Mumbai
   Mumbai

Question 1587 :
What will be the difference in time of two cities situated on the same longitude but one in northern and the other in southern hemisphere?

  1. No difference
  2. Four hours difference
  3. Four minutes difference
  4. There are no such cities
   No difference

Question 1588 :
Rotation of crops means

  1. Same crop is grown again and again
  2. Two crops are grown simultaneously to increase productivity of the soil
  3. Different crops are grown in succession to maintain the soil fertility
  4. None of the above
   Different crops are grown in succession to maintain the soil fertility

Question 1589 :
Black Gold' refers to

  1. Coal
  2. Petroleum
  3. Gold
  4. Diamond
   Petroleum

Question 1590 :
The first cement factory was built in India in 1904 at

  1. Ranchi
  2. Hazaribagh
  3. Chennai
