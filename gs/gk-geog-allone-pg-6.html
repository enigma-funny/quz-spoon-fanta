Question 151 :
As per 2011 census, the total number of census houses in Delhi city is around:

  1. 30 lakhs    
  2. 36 lakhs    
  3. 41 lakhs    
  4. 46 lakhs
  46 lakhs

Question 152 :
The Peninsular Plateau of India extends upto

  1. Mizo Hills
  2. Himachal Himalayas
  3. Assam Valley
  4. Maghalaya Hills
   Maghalaya Hills

Question 153 :
Developing Countries generate revenue mainly from:

  1. Agriculture Sector      
  2. Industrial Sector        
  3. Service Sector      
  4. None of these
  Service Sector

Question 154 :
The driest part of India is

  1. Rann of Kutch
  2. Rajasthan
  3. Leh
  4. Hinterland of Western Ghats

Question 155 :
In which zone, Outlying Business District is founded in Harris and Ullman’s Multiple-Nuclei Model of Urban Land Use?

  1. Zone I      
  2. Zone IV      
  3. Zone VII    
  4. Zone IX
  Zone VII

Question 156 :
Which of the following is recognised as a season by the meteorological department of India ?

  1. Cold weather
  2. Hot weather
  3. Retreating monsoon
  4. North-eastern monsoon
   North-eastern monsoon

Question 157 :
Based on the functional classification of cities of USA, Chauncey D. Harris classified the towns into:

  1. 3 categories    
  2. 6 categories    
  3. 9 categories    
  4. 12 categories
  9 categories

Question 158 :
The percentage of slums population from the total population in Aizawl city is around:

  1. 10%          
  2. 20%          
  3. 27%          
  4. 0.36
  0.27

Question 159 :
Gentrification in Urban Geography refers to:

  1. Set apart from each other; isolated or divided                      
  2. The process of repairing and rebuilding homes and businesses in a deteriorating area
  3. A part of a city, especially a slum area                        
  4. The process of growth of urban population                    
  The process of repairing and rebuilding homes and businesses in a deteriorating area

Question 160 :
The correct sequence of the eastward flowing rivers of the peninsular India from north to south is : [IAS 2002]

  1. Subarnarekha, Mahanadi, Godavari, Krishna, Pennar, Cauvery and Vagai
  2. Subarnarekha, Mahanadi, Krishna, Godavari, Cauvery, Vagai and Pennar
  3. Mahanadi, Subarnarekha, Godavari, Krishna, Cauvery, Pennar and Vagai
  4. Mahanadi, Subarnarekha, Krishna, Godavari, Cauvery, Vagai and Pennar
   Subarnarekha, Mahanadi, Godavari, Krishna, Pennar, Cauvery and Vagai

Question 161 :
When rain falls through a cold layer of air Dear the Earth's surface, raindrops get frozen into ice. This type of precipitation is called

  1. hail
  2. snowfall
  3. snowstorm
  4. sleet
   sleet

Question 162 :
The capacity of rock to allow water to pass through it is called

  1. hardness
  2. jointing
  3. porosity
  4. permeability
   permeability

Question 163 :
Give the reason behind Hoyt’s used Sector instead of circle shape in his Sectoral Model?

  1. Transportation routes radiating out from the city center which influence land values
  2. Easy for understanding                                  
  3. Land use patterns develop around a number of discrete centers                
  4. Simplicity
  Transportation routes radiating out from the city center which influence land values

Question 164 :
Which one of the following pairs is correctly matched? [IAS 1997]

  1. Teak : Jammu and Kashmir
  2. Deodar : Madhya Pradesh
  3. Sandalwood : Kerala
  4. Sundari : West Bengal
   Sandalwood : Kerala

Question 165 :
The temperature of Thiruvananthapuram is lower than that of Mumbai in May and higher than that of Mumbai in January, because: [IAS 2004]

  1. Thiruvananthapuram has cold current and Mumbai is faced with warm current
  2. Thiruvananthapuram has higher rainfall in summer and it is nearer to the equator
  3. Thiruvananthapuram is on the windward side and Mumbai is on the leeward side
  4. Thiruvananthapuram is Vegetated while Mumbai is not
   Thiruvananthapuram has higher rainfall in summer and it is nearer to the equator

Question 166 :

  1. Petrol      
  2. Water        
  3. Food    
  4. Beverage

Question 167 :
The National Highway - 1 connects Delhi to

  1. Chennai

Question 168 :
Spot the odd item in the following

  1. Mt Fuji

  3. Mt Vesuvius
  4. Mt Pele

Question 169 :
Growth of urban population is generally high in:

  1. Developed Countries  
  2. Developing Countries
  3. Depend on respective countries          
  4. None of these

Question 170 :
Major source of water in Delhi comes from the river of:

  1. Yamuna      
  2. Ganga        
  3. Tansa Lake      
  4. None of these
  Yamuna

Question 171 :
In which of the following cities of India the diurnal range of temperature is maximum?

  1. New Delhi
  2. Mumbai
  3. Chennai
  4. Kolkata
   New Delhi

Question 172 :
K = 7 hierarchy order implies:

  1. 1, 3, 5, 7….    
  2. 1, 7, 14, 21…    
  3. 1, 7, 49, 343…  
  4. None of these
  1, 7, 49, 343…

Question 173 :
The Red soils develop a reddish colour due to:

  1. deforestation and over-grazing
  2. the presence of potash and magnesia
  3. tropical monsoonal climate
  4. a wide diffusion of iron in ancient crystalline and metamorphic Jacks
   a wide diffusion of iron in ancient crystalline and metamorphic Jacks

Question 174 :
The ____ current is a cold water current.

  1. Gulf Stream
  2. Kuroshio
  3. Brazil
  4. Labrador
   Labrador

Question 175 :
Which one of the following layers of the atmosphere is responsible for the deflection of radio waves?

  1. Troposphere
  2. Stratosphere
  3. Mesosphere
  4. Ionosphere
   Ionosphere

Question 176 :
A huge muss of ice fed by snow on a high mountain slowly sliding downhill before It starts melting is called

  1. Glacier
  2. Avalanche
  3. Iceberg
  4. Snow cover
   Glacier

Question 177 :
Which of the following is not a product of the 'Monsoon Forests'?

  1. Teak
  2. Sal
  3. Olive
  4. Bamboo
   Olive

Question 178 :
Most of the floods in the lowlands of N. India, the Bihar plateau and Orissa are associated with:

  1. easterly depression
  2. orographic rainfall
  3. South-West Monsoon
  4. Thunderstorm
   orographic rainfall
