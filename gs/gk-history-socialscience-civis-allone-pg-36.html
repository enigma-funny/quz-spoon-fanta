Question 1051 :
The concept of Judicial Review in our Constitution has been taken from the Constitution of:

  1. England
  2. USA
  3. Canada
  4. Australia
  USA

Question 1052 :
Who of the following was the Permanent Chairman of the Constituent Assembly?

  1. Dr. B.R. Ambedkar
  2. Dr. Rajendra Prasad.
  3. Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru.
  4. Sardal Patel
  Dr. Rajendra Prasad.

Question 1053 :
In the field of religion Akbar's greatest contribution was in his

  1. founding of a new religion
  2. expansion of his father's religion
  3. the building of temples and mosques
  4. none of the above
  founding of a new religion

Question 1054 :
India borrowed the concept of 'sovereignty of parliament' from

  1. China
  2. Russia
  3. France
  4. Britain
  Britain

Question 1055 :
Who laid the foundation of Sri Harmandir Sahib at Amritsar?

  1. Guru Arjun Dev
  2. Mian Mir
  3. Guru Ram Das
  4. Bhai Gurdas
  Mian Mir

Question 1056 :
What is Gandhi's definition of Ram Raj?

  1. Sovereignty of the people based on pure moral authority
  2. The rule as it was during the time of Rama
  3. The greatest good of all
  4. The absolute power concentrated in the hands of a king
  Sovereignty of the people based on pure moral authority

Question 1057 :
Who was the last ruler of the Mughal Empire?

  1. Bahadur Shah II
  2. Aurangzeb
  3. Jahandar Shah
  4. Alamgir II

Question 1058 :
In the Mughal court which of these was the hall for public audience?

  2. Diwan-e-khas
  3. Hauz-khas
  4. Hauz-Aam

Question 1060 :
Who was the first foreign Minister of free India?

  1. Jawahar Lal Nehru
  2. Gulzari Lal Nehru
  3. Lal Bahadur Shastri
  4. John Mathai

Question 1061 :
If the finance Minister fails to get the Annual budget passed in the Lok Sabha, the Prime Minister is expected to —

  1. Compel the Finance Minister to resign
  2. Submit the resignation of his/ her cabinet
  3. Refer it to the Joint session of both the Houses of Parliament.
  4. Form another cabinet with different members
  Submit the resignation of his/ her cabinet

Question 1062 :
Who is considered to be responsible for the decline of the Mughal Empire?

  1. Ibrahim Lodhi
  2. Mohammad Tughlak
  3. Bahadur Shah Zafar
  4. Aurangzeb
  Aurangzeb

Question 1063 :
In a Parliamentary system the Executive is responsible:

  1. Directly to the people.
  2. To legislature.
  3. To judiciary.
  4. None of the above.
  To legislature.

Question 1064 :
The Prime Minister, Union Cabinet Minister, Chief Minister and Council of Ministers are all (members of

  1. National Development Council
  2. Inter State Council
  3. Planning Commission
  4. State Integration Council

Question 1065 :

  1. Akbar

  3. Jahangir
  4. Aurangzeb

Question 1066 :
What was the name of the Paramar king, who wielded the pen with no less dexterity than the sword?

  1. Siddharaja
  2. Bhoja
  3. Vakpati-Munja
  4. Krishnaraja

Question 1068 :

Question 1069 :
