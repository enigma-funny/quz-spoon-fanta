Question 2161 :
‘The Harbour Line’ of railway is running in the city of ___________________.

  Mumbai

Question 2162 :
Structural Analysis in Urban Geography has been based primarily on the work of _______________

  Karl Marx

Question 2163 :
Megacity has the total population more than _________________.

  10 million

Question 2164 :
Lesotho is:

  1. an island in Mediterranean Sea
  2. an important seaport in Tanzania
  3. a country completely surrounded by South Africa
  4. a mountain peak in Zambia
   a country completely surrounded by South Africa

Question 2165 :
Which is the smallest among the following water bodies?

  1. Arctic Sea
  2. Hudson Bay
  3. Japan Sea
  4. Okhotsk Sea
   Japan Sea

Question 2166 :
The average maximum distance people will travel to purchase goods and services is known as _____________________.

  Range of Goods or Services

Question 2167 :
An aerial unit having twenty-five or more kutcha structure mostly of temporary nature, or inhabited by persons with practically no private latrine and inadequate public latrine and water’ is known for __________________ slum.

  Undeclared

Question 2168 :
The concentric or zonal theory was propounded by _________________.

  Burgess

Question 2169 :
The movement of people out of cities to the surrounding areas is known as ______________________.

  Counter Urbanisation

Question 2170 :
The best planned city in India _______________.

  Chandigarh

Question 2171 :
The length of its day and the tilt of its axis are almost identical to those of the earth'. This is true of: [IAS 2003, 1994]

  1. Uranus
  2. Neptune
  3. Saturn
  4. Mars
   Mars

Question 2172 :
There are __________ (number) slum pockets are made by Government of Mizoram in Aizawl city.

  73

Question 2173 :
The international air route from West Europe to East Asia passes through India because:

  1. India is a busy centre situated between the two places
  2. it provides the shortest route
  3. India is the only country in Asia providing refuelling capacity
  4. None of these
   India is a busy centre situated between the two places

Question 2174 :
Metropolitan city has the total population more than _________________.

  1 (one) million

Question 2175 :
Sargasso sea is located in :

  1. North Pacific Ocean
  2. North Atlantic Ocean
  3. North Sea
  4. South Atlantic Ocean
   North Atlantic Ocean

Question 2176 :
There are ______________ number of towns in Mizoram.

  23

Question 2177 :
Census town must have a density of population more than _______________.

  400 persons/sq. km

Question 2178 :
The division of developed and developing countries is mainly based on _______________.

  Economy

Question 2179 :
The countries which are facing the beginning of industrialization are called ____________.

  Developing countries

Question 2180 :
Oolong tea is: [IAS 2003]

  1. a scented tea
  2. tea cultivated in home gardens
  3. a variety of tea cultivated in Taiwan
  4. green leaf tea of China
   a variety of tea cultivated in Taiwan

Question 2181 :
The concept of Rank Size Rule was proposed in the year of ______________

  1949

Question 2182 :
The expansion of poorly planned, low-density, auto-dependent development, which spreads out over large amounts of land is known as ________________________.

  Urban Sprawl

Question 2183 :
The ‘Multiple Nuclei Theory of Town Planning’ was given by ___________________.

  Ullman

Question 2184 :
 The phenomenon ‘Urban revolution’ was mainly led by ___________________.

  Industrial Revolution

Question 2185 :
What is the administrative capital of South Africa? [Railways 1994]

  1. Cape Town
  2. Johannesburg
  3. Kimberley
  4. Pretoria
   Pretoria

Question 2186 :
Coral reefs are formed by : [IAS 2004]

  1. volcanic rocks
  2. marine sediments
  3. chlorine material precipitated from sea water
  4. tiny colonial marine animals which construct limestone skeleton material
   tiny colonial marine animals which construct limestone skeleton material

Question 2187 :
The phrase of ‘There is no real world’ is related to the approach of ___________________.

  Postmodernist Approach

Question 2188 :
Major source of water in Aizawl comes from the river of __________________.

  Tlawng

Question 2189 :
The altitudes of heavenly bodies appear to be greater than they actually are. This is due to:

  1. vertical motion of these heavenly bodies
  2. motions of the earth
  3. atmospheric refraction
  4. these are visible because of light of the sun reflected from their surface
   atmospheric refraction

Question 2190 :
_______________________ is one most influence the land and rent values in Hoyt Sectoral Model.

  Transportation Routes
