Question 31 :
Coniferous forests are in the region of:

  1. high humidity and high temperature
  2. low rainfall and very low temperature throughout the year
  3. low mean annual temperature throughout the year and very cold winter
  4. heavy rainfall and high temperature during summer
   low rainfall and very low temperature throughout the year

Question 32 :
The zones of excessively dry climate with very cold temperature throughout the year correspond to: [IES 1993]

  1. Alpine meadows
  2. Antarctica
  3. Arctic deserts
  4. Tundra
   Tundra

Question 38 :
Though coffee and tea both are cultivated on hill slopes, there is some difference between them regarding their cultivation. In this context, consider the following statements: 1. Coffee plant requires a hot and humid climate of tropical areas whereas tea can be cultivated in both tropical and subtropical areas. 2. Coffee is propagated by seeds but tea is propagated by stem cuttings only. Which of the statements given above is/are correct? [IAS 2010]

  1. 1 only
  2. 2 only
  3. Both 1 and 2
  4. Neither 1 nor 2
   1 only

Question 39 :
The tropical rain forests are dense and varied because of: [CDS 1994]

  1. their remote and inaccessible locations
  2. very little interference from man
  3. an abundance of moisture and warm temperature throughout the year
  4. poor economic development
   an abundance of moisture and warm temperature throughout the year

Question 43 :

Question 47 :
No trees are found in Tundra biome near polar region of northern hemisphere. This is due to : [NDA 2011]

  1. snowfall inhibits plant respiration
  2. frozen ice beneath the surface soil (permafrost) restricts root growth
  3. less wind movement and inadequate sunlight
  4. low temperature which restricts development of reproductive organs
