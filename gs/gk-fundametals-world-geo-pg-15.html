Question 421 :
The Savannas or Orinoco Basin are termed as

  1. pampas
  2. steppes
  3. prairies
  4. llanos
   llanos

Question 422 :
The countries which are called the 'Rice Bowl' of Asia are

  1. China, Japan, Sri Lanka, India
  2. Myanmar, Thailand, Kampuchea and Vietnam
  3. India, Pakistan, Nepal, Mongolia
  4. Japan, Indonesia, Malaysia, India
   Myanmar, Thailand, Kampuchea and Vietnam

Question 423 :
The total length of the oceanic ridge systems is of the order of

  1. 40000 km
  2. 65000 km
  3. 95000 km
  4. 150000 km
   65000 km

Question 424 :
Imaginary lines connecting places in oceans having equal salinity are called

  1. Isotherms
  2. Isobars
  3. Isohalines
  4. Isohyets
   Isohalines

Question 425 :
Plains formed by the deposits brought by glacier are

  1. lacustrine plains
  2. flood plains
  3. drift plains
  4. coastal plains
   drift plains

Question 426 :
Hurricanes or typhoons (tropical cyclones) develop and mature

  1. over land bodies only
  2. in a belt 35°-65° N and S latitudes
  3. anywhere in the tropical zone
  4. over water bodies only
   over water bodies only

Question 429 :

Question 430 :
In the Himalayas the melting or the snow is more on the

  1. southern slopes
  2. northern slopes
  3. eastern slopes
  4. western slopes
   southern slopes

Question 431 :
A strait is

  1. a narrow stretch of sea connecting two extensive areas of sea
  2. a narrow strip of land joining two large land-masses
  3. a wide indentation into the land formed by a sea or a lake
  4. a narrow headland
   a narrow stretch of sea connecting two extensive areas of sea

Question 432 :
Venus has atmosphere made up of

  1. Ammonia
  2. Carbon-di-Oxide

Question 441 :
What is the usual altitude of the plains?

  1. Sea level
  2. Less than 500 metres above sea level
  3. Less than 600 metres above sea level
  4. Less than 800 metres above sea level

Question 442 :
During the early period of Industrial Revolution Industries were located primarily

  1. near major ports
  2. on the banks of navigable rivers
  3. near coal fields
  4. near nodal centres
   near coal fields

Question 443 :
Why does temperature increase with increase in height in Stratosphere?

  1. Closeness of the sun
  2. Decrease in atmospheric pressure
  3. Concentration of ozone
  4. None of these
   Concentration of ozone

Question 444 :
The term Mediterranean agriculture applies to a particular blend of ______ and activities and ______is confined to regions encircling the Mediterranean basins.

  1. farming and grazing
  2. farming and fishing
  3. grazing and fishing
  4. fishing and mining

  2. Michigan

  4. Erie

Question 448 :
An estuary is a place where

  1. deposits of mud can be seen at a triangular piece of land
