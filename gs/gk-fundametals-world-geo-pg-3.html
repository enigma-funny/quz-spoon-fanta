  1. forest

Question 73 :

  1. mineral

  3. soil

Question 74 :
The north flowing rivers of Siberia are not of much use tor navigation mainly because

  1. they are too shallow in the dry season
  2. the lower courses remain frozen for many months
  3. they flow through uninhabited areas
  4. they lack navigation facilities to ensure safety
