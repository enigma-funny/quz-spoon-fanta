Question 1084 :

Question 1086 :
99. Which one of the following statements about the Indus culture is the least possible?

  1. The Indus culture originated from the Kalibanga I culture
  2. The Indus culture originated from the peasant cultures of sind - Bluchistan
  3. The Indus culture is an expression of Sothi culture
  4. The Indus culture sprang directly as an urban culture
   The Indus culture originated from the peasant cultures of sind - Bluchistan

Question 1098 :
