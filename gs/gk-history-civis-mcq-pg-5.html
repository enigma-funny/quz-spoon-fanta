Question 121 :
The first Indian state which was created on a linguistic basis following a long drawn agitation is:

  1. Andhra Pradesh
  2. Assam
  3. Tamil Nadu
  4. Karnataka
  Andhra Pradesh

Question 122 :
Chief Minister of a State is responsible and answerable to

  1. Prime Minister
  2. Rajya Sabha
  3. Legislative Assembly (Vidhan Sabha)
  4. Governor

Question 127 :

  1. the 38th Amendment act, 1975

Question 128 :

  3. Article 51 A

  1. Cabinet Ministers, Minister of State and Deputy Ministers
  2. Cabinet Ministers and Chief Ministers of the States
  3. Prime Minister and Cabinet Ministers

The Unitary system of Government possesses which of the following advantages?

  1. Greater adaptability
  2. Strong State
  3. Greater participation by the people
  4. Lesser chances of authoritarianism

Question 134 :
The Constitution of India describes India as:

  1. A federation.
  2. Quasi-federal.
  3. A Union of States.
  4. None of these.

Question 136 :
The President of India is elected by the numbers of an electoral college which does NOT include —

  1. The elected members of the Rajya Sabha
  2. The elected members of the Rajya Sabha
  3. The elected members of the state assemblies
  4. The elected members of the state legislative councils

Question 137 :

  1. the Preamble.
  2. the Chapter of Fundamental Rights.
  3. the Chapter on Directive Principles of State Policy.
  4. all of the above.

Question 139 :
Which one of the following is in correct order in the Preamble of the Indian Constitution?

  1. Sovereign, Socialist, Secular, Democratic, Republic.

Question 145 :
