Question 1240 :
Under the Constitution of India, which one of the following is NOT a fundamental duty?

  1. To vote in public elections
  2. To develop the scientific temper
  3. To safeguard public property
  4. To abide by the Constitution and respect its deals

Question 1241 :
The salary and allowances of the Chief Minister is determined by

  1. President
  2. Governor

  4. State Legislature

Question 1242 :

  1. The speaker of the Lok Sabha

  3. The Minister for Parliamentary Affairs
  4. The Election Commission of India

Question 1243 :
The basic features of the Indian Constitution which are not amendable under Article 368 are —

  1. Sovereignty, territorial integrity, federal system and judicial review
  2. Sovereignty, territorial Integrity and parliamentary system of Government
  3. Judicial review and the federal system
  4. Sovereignty, territorial integrity, federal system, judicial review and parliamentary system of government
  Sovereignty, territorial integrity, federal system, judicial review and parliamentary system of government

Question 1244 :
The Peacock Throne was made during the reign of

Question 1245 :
Marriage of one man with several sisters are called-

  3. Non-Sororal polygyny
  4. Levirate

Question 1246 :
Which of the following is correct regarding the Governor of a State?

  1. He can recommend to the President to impose President's Rule in the State.
  2. No money bill can be introduced in the State Legislature without his prior permission.
  3. He has the power of issuing ordinances when the legislature is not in session.
  4. All of the above

The Panchayati Raj Administration is primarily aimed:
