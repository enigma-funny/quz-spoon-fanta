Question 61 :
Which of the following is/are correct about the Rann of Kachchh ? 1.It is a flat saline marsh land 2.It is in Rajasthan 3.It is the home of the Indian Wild Ass 4.It is a unique land formation supporting several singular species of fauna

  1. 1, 2 and 3
  2. 2, 3 and 4
  3. 1, 3 and 4
  4. 1, 2, 3 and 4
   1, 3 and 4

Question 62 :
To which group do the tribals of central and southern India belong?

  1. Proto-Australoids
  2. Negritos
  3. Mongoloids
  4. Brachycephals
   Proto-Australoids

Question 63 :

Question 64 :
If the usual rule of calculation of time were applied for the extremes of Gujrat and Arunachal Pradesh, then approximately time: [CPO AC 2003]

  1. at Gujarat would be ahead of that at Arunachal Pradesh by about 3 hours
  2. at Gujarat would be ahead of that at Arunachal Pradesh by about 2 hours
  3. at Arunachal Pradesh would be ahead of that at Gujarat by about 3 hours

longitude is geographically significant to India because: [CPO SI 2003]
