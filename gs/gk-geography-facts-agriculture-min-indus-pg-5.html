Question 127 :

Question 128 :

Question 129 :

Question 141 :
The temperature decreases towards poles, because :

  1. at poles the snow does not melt
  2. the cold regions do not absorb sun rays
  3. the movement of cold wind is towards poles

Question 146 :
Which one of the following characteristics is NOT found in the Mediterranean climate? [NDA 2003]

  1. Temperatures range from 21°C in summer to 10°C or below in the winter
  2. Off-shore trade winds blow in the summer
  3. On-shore westerly winds blow in the winter bringing cyclonic rain
  4. The annual rainfall ranges from 1500 mm to 2000 mm
   Off-shore trade winds blow in the summer

Question 148 :
Many Savanna regions are unable to support good crops because:

  1. they have large tall grasses which form a canopy over the good crops and do not allow them to grow
  2. there are large number of animals in this region, which eat up the crops
  3. the climate is too hot for the crops to grow
  4. they have poor lateritic soil
   they have large tall grasses which form a canopy over the good crops and do not allow them to grow

Question 149 :

Question 150 :
The Himalayas have snowline at 4300 m whereas in the Alps mountain, it is at 3000 m. This is because the: [CDS 1994]
