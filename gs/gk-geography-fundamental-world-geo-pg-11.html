Question 301 :
Which of the following is not one of the by-products of mineral oil?

  1. Naphtha
  2. Sulphur
  3. Aniline dyes
  4. Paraffin wax

Question 302 :
Canals serve as passage of communication in the city of ___in Italy.

  1. Rome
  2. Venice
  3. Turin
  4. Genoa
   Venice

Question 303 :
A huge muss of ice fed by snow on a high mountain slowly sliding downhill before It starts melting is called

  1. Glacier
  2. Avalanche
  3. Iceberg

  1. intensive cultivation
  2. extensive cultivation
  3. mono cultivation

Question 311 :

  1. saturation temperature
  2. critical temperature
  3. condensation temperature

Question 312 :
There are two types of crusts – the continental and the oceanic. The continental crust is

  1. thicker below the plains than below mountains
  2. thicker below the mountains than below the plains
  3. almost same all over
  4. thicker under the oceans than under the seas

Question 313 :
Suez Canal links

  1. Mediterranean Sea and Caribbean Sea
  2. Black Sea and Baltic Sea
  3. Red Sea and Mediterranean Sea

Question 323 :
The wettest place in the world is Cherrapunji (Assam). The Hottest place is

  1. Azizia (Libya)
  2. Ramagundam (India)
  3. Stwart Island (New Zealand)
  4. Kandy (Sri Lanka)

Question 324 :
Which of the statements as regards the consequences of the movement of the earth is not correct?

  1. Revolution of the earth is the cause of the change of seasons
  2. Rotation of the earth is the cause of days and nights
  3. Rotation of the earth cause variation in the duration of days and nights
  4. Rotation of the earth affects the movement of winds and ocean currents
   Rotation of the earth cause variation in the duration of days and nights

Question 325 :
When dew point is reached it is said that at that temperature

  1. the atmosphere is said to be saturated with water vapour
  2. the relative humidity of the atmosphere is 100%

  4. All the above
