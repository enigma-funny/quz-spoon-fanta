Question 1471 :
The youngest rivers in India originate from

  1. Eastern Ghats
  2. Western Ghats
  3. Himalayas
  4. Deccan Plateau
   Himalayas

Question 1472 :
Latex is a name for

  1. a milk product
  2. latent heat
  3. crude rubber
  4. refined honey
   crude rubber

Question 1473 :
Limnology refers to the study of

  1. Limestone
  2. Lignite of brown coal
  3. Lakes and ponds
  4. Laterites
   Lakes and ponds

Question 1474 :
The longest distance covered by one Broad Gauge train in India is between

  1. Kanniyakumari and Jammu Tawi
  2. Guwahati and Tiruvananthpuram
  3. New Delhi and Chennai
  4. Gorakhpur and Cochin
   Kanniyakumari and Jammu Tawi

Question 1475 :
Which of the following is a new bio-fertilizers?

  1. Green Blue Algae
  2. Nitrates
  3. Compost
  4. Farmyard manure
   Green Blue Algae

Question 1476 :
The highest peak in India is

  1. Mt Everest
  2. Kanchenjunga
  3. Mt Godwin Austin (K2)
  4. Dhaulagiri
   Mt Godwin Austin (K2)

Question 1477 :
The commodity which accounts for the maximum volume of goods transported by the railways in India is

  1. iron and steel
  2. coal
  3. mineral oil
  4. metallic ores (other than iron ore)
   coal

Question 1478 :
Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) is in

  1. Mumbai
  2. Kolkata
  3. Bengaluru
  4. Chennai
   Bengaluru

Question 1479 :
The driest part in India is in

  1. Jaisalmer (Western Rajasthan)
  2. Bhatinda (Punjab)
  3. Darbhanga (Bihar)
  4. Gandhinagar (Gujarat)
   Jaisalmer (Western Rajasthan)

Question 1480 :
Which of the following countries has the greatest length of inland waterways?

  1. The United States
  2. The Former USSR
  3. Germany
  4. France
   The Former USSR

Question 1481 :
Which of the following States is called the Garden of Spices?

  1. Karnataka
  2. Kerala
  3. Andhra Pradesh
  4. Tamil Nadu
   Kerala

Question 1482 :
Which of the following States does not share the drainage basin of the Krishna?

  1. Maharashtra
  2. Karnataka
  3. Andhra Pradesh
  4. Tamil Nadu
   Tamil Nadu

Question 1483 :
The Himalayas are formed of parallel fold range a of which the oldest range is

  1. the Shiwalik Range
  2. the Lesser Himalayas
  3. the Great Himalayan Range
  4. the Dhaula Dhar Range
   the Great Himalayan Range

Question 1484 :
There is no Scheduled Tribe population in

  1. Kerala
  2. Tamil Nadu
  3. Punjab
  4. Rajasthan
   Punjab

Question 1485 :
The Ganga plain widens from

  1. south to north
  2. west to east
  3. north to south
  4. east to west
   east to west

Question 1486 :
Which State in India is known as the 'Granary of India'?

  1. Haryana
  2. Andhra Pradesh
  3. Punjab
  4. Kerala
   Punjab

Question 1487 :
Lucerene is a

  1. Cereal crop
  2. Cash crop
  3. Foliage crop
  4. Fibre crop
   Foliage crop

Question 1488 :
Which of the following non-conventional sources of energy holds out the maximum promise in India?

  1. Solar Energy
  2. Geothermal Energy
  3. Tidal Energy
  4. Wind Energy
   Solar Energy

Question 1489 :
The city located on the river Yamuna is

  1. Gujrat
  2. Lucknow
  3. Patna
  4. Agra
   Agra

Question 1490 :
Which of the following is NOT a plant product?

  1. Aloe vera
  2. Vanilla
  3. Kiwi
  4. All are plant products
   All are plant products

Question 1491 :
Operation Flood refers to

  1. Blue revolution
  2. Green revolution
  3. White revolution
  4. All the three
   White revolution

Question 1492 :
Through which of the following sets of states does Krishna river flow?

  1. Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh
  2. Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh
  3. Maharashtra, Andhra Pradesh
  4. Maharashtra, Karnataka
   Maharashtra, Karnataka

Question 1493 :
Which one among the following has the largest shipyard In India?

  1. Kolkata
  2. Kochi
  3. Mumbai
  4. Visakhapatnam
   Kochi

Question 1494 :
Tobacco was introduced into India by the

  1. Chinese
  2. Portuguese
  3. English
  4. French
   Portuguese

Question 1495 :
The Indus Valley Civilization was based on the racial stocks of

  1. Mediterraneans
  2. Nordics
  3. Negritos
  4. None of the above
   Mediterraneans

Question 1496 :
India has an endless growing season because

  1. it experiences the monsoonal type of climate
  2. the distribution of rainfall is uneven
  3. temperatures throughout the year are favourable for growth of plants
  4. its soil is very fertile the greater part being alluvial
   temperatures throughout the year are favourable for growth of plants

Question 1497 :
HBJ pipe line is being laid for a distance of 1730 km in order to

  1. provide cooking gas for villages
  2. provide employment for rural population
  3. provide feedstock for six large fertilisers
  4. serve the power requirements of many states
   provide feedstock for six large fertilisers

Question 1498 :
Where is the MIG engine manufactured in India?

  1. Koraput
  2. Bengaluru
  3. Ozar
  4. Hyderabad
   Koraput

Question 1499 :
Which one of the following gives the two largest Union Territories of India (in terms of geographical area) in the correct descending order?

  1. Andaman and Nicobar Islands and Delhi
  2. Chandigarh, Lakshadweep
  3. Delhi, Puducherry
  4. Delhi, Chandigarh
   Andaman and Nicobar Islands and Delhi

Question 1500 :
Which of the following fertilisers provides maximum amount of nitrogen?

  1. Calcium Nitrate
  2. Urea
  3. Ammonium Sulphate
  4. Nitrolium
   Urea
