Question 1941 :
The four varnas are mentioned for the first time in the—

  1. The people were not settled permanently in one locality
  2. The expenditure of the government was low
  3. The king was not considered as the proprietor of the land
  4. The people were not accustomed to paying land tax

Question 1942 :

  1. Pt. Jawaharlal Nehru
  2. Dr. Rajendra Prasad
  3. Mahatma Gandhi
