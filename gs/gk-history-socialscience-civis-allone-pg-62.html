  2. Faience
  3. Agate

Question 1838 :

  1. They were atheists
  2. They were welcomed by the vaisyas
  3. They advocated ahimsa
  4. They believed in rigorous asceticism

Question 1839 :

Question 1840 :

  2. 1917
  3. 1937
  4. Globalization

Question 1841 :

  1. Kudoor
  2. Kanchipuram
  3. Vengadam

Question 1842 :
Which historian brought out the interdisciplinary method to study ancient indian history?

  2. D.D. Kosambi
  3. R.S.Sharma
  4. D.N.Jha

Question 1843 :
The leaders of 1857 Mutineers who was caught - hanged was —

  1. Kunwar Singh
  2. Nana Saheb
  3. Tantya Tope
  4. Rani Laxmibai of Jhansi

Question 1844 :
Among the officers of Muhhammad of Ghor, Bihar and part of Bengal were conquered by—

  1. The Sthaviravada sect of Buddhism
  2. The Mahasangbika sect of Buddhism
  3. The Shwetamber sect of Jainism
  4. Purva-Mimamsakas

Question 1845 :
Which Bhakti Saint is known as the bridge between north and south?

  1. 4, 3, 2, 1
  2. 4, 2, 3, 1
  3. 1, 2, 3, 4
  4. 3, 2, 1, 4
   1, 2, 3, 4

Question 1846 :
Dadabhai Nauroji, the greatest exponent of the theory of ‘Drain of wealth’ called the economic exploitation of India—

  1. Evil of all evils
  2. A continuous fleecing
  3. A continuous British invasion
  4. None of these
   A continuous British invasion

Question 1847 :
Hieroglyphic script meant

  3. AD 652

Question 1848 :

Question 1849 :

  3. Yaudheyas

Question 1850 :
Ptolemy built the best museum in

  1. Arts
  2. History
  3. Geography
  4. Metallurgy

Question 1851 :
In which age Brahmanas were inferior than Kshatriyas?

  1. Vedic age
  2. Buddha age
  3. Maurya age
  4. Post-Mauryan age

Question 1852 :
The Chola ruler who wrested tondaimandalam from the Rashtrakutas was—

  1. Language of the people was used
  2. No caste barriers
  3. Non-existence of sound
  4. Too much stress on morality
   Too much stress on morality

Question 1853 :
Which one of the following Sultans extended agricultural loans called sondhars to the poor peasants for promoting cultivation?

  1. Balban
  2. Alauddin Khalji
  3. Mohd. Bin Tughlaq
  4. Firoze Tughlaq
   Mohd. Bin Tughlaq

Question 1854 :
According to the Mahasanghikas, a being is composed of—

  1. . Five Dharmas
  2. Seven Dharmas
  3. Nine Dharmas
  4. Eighteen Dharmas
  . Five Dharmas

Question 1855 :
Which of the following regions was were well-known during the seventeenth century for simultaneous production of saltpetre, indigo and opium?

  1. Both (a) and (R) are true and (R) is the correct explanation of (a)
  2. Both (a) and (R) are true but (R) is not a correct explanation of (a)
  3. (a) is true but (R) is false
  4. (a) is false but (R) is true
   Both (a) and (R) are true but (R) is not a correct explanation of (a)

Question 1856 :
The Forward Bloc was formed by

  1. Kesari – B. G. Tilak
  2. New India – M. K. Gandhi
  3. Young India – Annie Besant
  4. Yugantar – Aurobindo Ghosh

Question 1857 :
The first Turkish Sultan to cross Vindhyachal ranges was?

  1. Iltutmish
  2. Balban
  3. Alauddin Khilji
  4. Firoz Shah Tughlaq

Question 1858 :
Indian Economic drain was described in the British Period by—

  1. Dadabhai Nauroji
  2. Surendra Nath Beneraji
