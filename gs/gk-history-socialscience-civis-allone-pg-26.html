Question 758 :
Which one of the following statements correctly describes the – Dual Government arrangement made by the East-India Company in Bengal?

  1. Executive and judicial authority were separated
  2. Revenue collection was entrusted to the Deputy Nawab, while military affairs were retained by the Company
  3. Revenue administration and the administration of criminal justice were separated and were with the British and the Nawab respectively
  4. The new arrangement was made to encourage agriculture
   Revenue collection was entrusted to the Deputy Nawab, while military affairs were retained by the Company

Question 770 :
Which of the following statements is false with regard to the social conditions in the Gupta period?

  1. Public and personal morality had reached a very low point
  2. The caste system was liberalised
  3. The living standard of the common people was simple but the town life was glamorous and sophisticated
  4. The system of devadasis was in vogue during this period
   Public and personal morality had reached a very low point
