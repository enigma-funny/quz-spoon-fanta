Question 331 :
Which of the following is/are correct? 1. Broad guage railway engines- Varanasi 2. Electric locomotives - Bhopal

  1. 1
  2. 2
  3. 3
  4. 1, 2 and 3
   1, 2 and 3

Question 332 :
Which one of the following statements is correct? [NDA 2003]

  1. India ranks 9th in the world's shipping tonnage
  2. Mumbai port handles half of the country's foreign trade
  3. Kandla and Kolkata are tidal ports
  4. India has 14 major ports and 129 minor ports
   Mumbai port handles half of the country's foreign trade

Question 333 :
The Himalayan Range is very rich in species diversity. Which one among the following is the most appropriate reason -for this phenomenon? [CSAT 2011]

  1. It has a high rainfall that supports luxuriant vegetative growth
  2. It is a confluence of different bio-geographical zones
  3. Exotic and invasive species have not been introduced in this region
  4. It has less human interference
   It is a confluence of different bio-geographical zones

Question 334 :
Ozone layer is present in

  1. Stratosphere
  2. Troposphere
  3. Ionosphere
  4. Exosphere
   Stratosphere

Question 335 :
The Indian Wild Ass (Ghor-Khur) is found in:

  1. Sunderbans
  2. Assam forests
  3. the Rann of Kachch
  4. Cauvery delta
   the Rann of Kachch

Question 336 :
Consider the following statements: 1. Tamil Nadu is the only area which receives about half of its rainfall from north-east monsoon winds. 2. Kerala is the only state receiving very heavy rainfall from the south-west monsoon winds. 3. In India, the south-west monsoon is stronger than the north-east monsoon. Which of these are correct statements? [NDA 2001]

  1. 1, 2 and 3
  2. 1 and 2
  3. 1 and 3
  4. 2 and 3
   1, 2 and 3

Question 337 :
Which one of the following statements on biosphere reserves is not correct? [CDS 2010]

  1. In 1973, UNESCO launched a worldwide programme on man and biosphere
  2. Biosphere reserves promote research on ecological conservation
  3. Nanda Devi Biosphere Reserve is located in Madhya Pradesh
  4. Biosphere reserves are multipurpose protected areas to preserve the genetic diversity in ecosystems
   Nanda Devi Biosphere Reserve is located in Madhya Pradesh

Question 338 :

  2. 4, 3, 2, 1
  3. 3, 1, 4, 2
  4. 2, 4, 1, 3

Question 342 :
Which one of the following is the correct sequence of the given tiger reserves of India from north of south? [CDS 2010]

  1. I and II
  2. I, II and III
  3. II and III
  4. I and III

Question 344 :

  1. Krishna serves Maharashtra and Andhra Pradesh

Question 349 :

Question 350 :
Which one of the following is the most possible solution for planning improvement of a metropolitan city in India? [IAS 2003]

  1. Constructing high rise building and sky scrapers
  2. Dispersing the factories - industries to the neighbouring but independent location.
  3. Building up suburbs and satellite towns around the main city
  4. Preventing immigration of people into the metropolitan city
   Dispersing the factories - industries to the neighbouring but independent location.
