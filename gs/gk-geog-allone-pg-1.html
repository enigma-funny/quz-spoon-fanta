Question 1 :
Which of the following States is least developed in rail routes? [NDA 2002]

  1. Chhattisgarh
  2. Jharkhand
  3. Manipur
  4. Uttaranchal
   Manipur

Question 2 :
Put in descending order of area with the following soils in India: 1. Red soils 2. Laterite soils 3. Alluvial soils

  1. I, IV, II, III
  2. IV, II, I, III
  3. IV, I, III, II
  4. I, III, IV, II
   I, IV, II, III

  2. 2000 km.        
  3. 3000 km.

Coastal Andhra Pradesh and Orissa often face natural disasters due to: [CPO SI 2003]

  1. cyclones
  2. earthquakes
  3. landslides
  4. tornadoes

Question 5 :
Meaning of conurbation:

  1. The process of growth of urban population                        
  2. The study of the life of the city                                
  3. The movement of people out of cities to the surrounding areas                
  4. A territorial coalescence of two or more cities whose peripheral zones have grown together
  A territorial coalescence of two or more cities whose peripheral zones have grown together

Question 6 :
The point below the surface where an earthquake originates is called ____.

  1. Tsunamis
  2. Focus
  3. Epicentre
  4. Eye of the Earthquake
   Focus

Question 7 :
Among the following regions of India, which one receives comparatively least amount of annual rainfall? [IAS 2009]

  1. An 80 km wide coastal belt from Nellore to Point Calimere
  2. The middle and lower Asom valley
  3. North-eastern Rajasthan
  4. The coastal plains of Gujarat south of Narmada
   North-eastern Rajasthan

Question 8 :

  1. The Nile
  2. The Niger
  3. The Congo
  4. The Zambezi
   The Zambezi

Question 9 :
Meaning of Counter Urbanisation:

  1. The process of growth of urban population                        
  2. The study of the life of the city                                
  3. The movement of people out of cities to the surrounding areas                
  4. A territorial coalescence of two or more cities whose peripheral zones have grown together
  The movement of people out of cities to the surrounding areas

Question 10 :
The phenomenon known as the Midnight Sun is caused by

  1. The rotation of the Earth on its axis
  2. Sun spots
  3. Inclination of the Earth's axis
  4. Nuclear explosions
   Inclination of the Earth's axis

Question 11 :
M6nsoon retreat is marked by: [UP PCS 1995] 1. clear sky 2. high pressure conditions at the Bay of Bengal 3. rise in temperature on land

  1. 1 only
  2. 1 and 2
  3. 2 and 3
  4. 1, 2 and 3
   1 only

Question 12 :
Urbanism is mainly the study of:

  1. Process of urban growth          
  2. Growth of the urban population      
  3. the character of city life          
  4. None of these
  the character of city life

Question 13 :
The countries which are facing the beginning of industrialization are called:

  1. Developed Countries      
  2. Developing Countries            
  3. Less Developed Countries    
  4. None of these

Question 14 :
The north-western India receives substantial rainfall during winter months because of:

  1. south-west monsoons
  2. retreating monsoons
  3. north-east monsoons
  4. westerly disturbances
