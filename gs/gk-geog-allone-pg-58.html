Question 1711 :
______ Oilfield of India has the largest quantity of recoverable reserves.

  1. Moran Oilfield
  2. Mumbai High (off shore) Oilfield
  3. Ankaleshwar Oilfield
  4. Naharkatiya Oilfield
   Mumbai High (off shore) Oilfield

Question 1712 :
Which of the following physiographic features of India is 'geologically the oldest?

  1. Himalayan Mountain Chain
  2. Indus-Ganga Brahmaputra Plain
  3. The Peninsular Plateau
  4. The Coastal Plains
   The Peninsular Plateau

Question 1713 :
The term used for the zone at low pressure and light and variable winds in the oceanic equatorial regions is :

  1. depression
  2. doldrums
  3. horse-latitudes
  4. inter-tropical convergence
   depression

Question 1714 :

  1. 1 only
  2. 2 only
  3. Both 1 and 2
  4. Neither 1 nor 2

Question 1738 :
Which of the following indicates the types of soil erosion in decreasing order of damage caused In India

  1. Ravine erosion .and gullies, alkalinity and salinity weeds and water-logging
  2. Alkalinity and water-logging, ravine erosion and weeds
  3. Water-logging, weeds, salinity and ravine erosion
  4. All are equally damaging

Question 1739 :
There is heavy rainfall on the western coast of India but very little in the Deccan because

  1. the Deccan plateau is situated in the rain shadow of the Western Ghats
  2. the region is bypassed by the south-west monsoons
  3. lack of high mountains in the Deccan
  4. of some unknown reason
