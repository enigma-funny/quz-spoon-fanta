Question 2191 :
As per 2011 census, the total population of Aizawl city is around ______________ lakhs.

  3

Question 2192 :
As per 2011 census, the total population of Mumbai’s Urban Agglomeration is about ________________.

  20 million

Question 2193 :
The Urban Community' (1926) was written by _________________.

  E. Burgess

Question 2194 :
With increasing distance from the center of the town; population density usually ________________.

  Decrease

Question 2195 :
The concept of The Multiple Nuclei Model was proposed in the year of _______________.

  1945

Question 2196 :
The process of repairing and rebuilding homes and businesses in a deteriorating area is known as __________________.

  Gentrification

Question 2197 :
In order of their distances from the Sun, which of the following planets lie between Mars and Uranus? [IAS 2008]

  1. Earth and Jupiter
  2. Jupiter and Saturn
  3. Saturn and Earth
  4. Saturn and Neptune
   Jupiter and Saturn

Question 2198 :
The term 'meteor' is applied to an interplanetary body:

  1. after it enters the atmosphere of the earth
  2. after it enters the earth's atmosphere and explodes in mid air as a ball of fire
  3. after it enters the earth's atmosphere and lands on the surface of the earth without exploding in mid-air
  4. before it enters the earth's atmosphere
   after it enters the earth's atmosphere and explodes in mid air as a ball of fire

Question 2199 :
The 'Water table' refers to :

  1. the lower limit of the zone of saturation
  2. the upper limit of the zone of saturation
  3. the contact zone of permeable and impermeable rocks
  4. seepage of water into fissures lying below the earth's surface
   the upper limit of the zone of saturation

Question 2200 :
The name of the largest slum area in India is __________________.

  Dharavi

Question 2201 :
The concept of Wedge or Sectoral Model was proposed in the year of _______________.

  1939

Question 2202 :
The distance of the equator from either of the poles is :

  1. 500 km
  2. 5001 km
  3. 10,002 km
  4. 36,005 km
   10,002 km

Question 2203 :
Full form of BSUP is _____________________________________.

  Basic Service to Urban Poor.

Question 2204 :
An area should be identified personally by the Charge Officer and also inspected by an officer nominated by the Director of Census operation. Such areas are considered as ________________ slums.

  Identified

Question 2205 :
Which one the following condition is not favourable for the formation of delta? [IAS 2003]

  1. Active vertical and lateral erosion in the lower course of the rivers
  2. Coast should be preferably tideless
  3. Sea adjoining the delta should be shallow
  4. There should be no strong current running at right angles to the river mouths
   Active vertical and lateral erosion in the lower course of the rivers

Question 2206 :
Stars which appear single to the naked eye but are double stars when observed by a telescope, are known as :

  1. cosmic stars
  2. quasars
  3. binaries
  4. novae and supernovae
   binaries

Question 2207 :
The Earth rotates-around an axis pointing towards: [NDA 1991]

  1. the moon
  2. the pole star
  3. the sun
  4. Venus
   the pole star

Question 2208 :
Developing Countries generate revenue mainly from __________________ sector.

  Service

Question 2209 :
Major source of water in Delhi comes from the river of ___________________.

  Yamuna

Question 2210 :
The basic transport network of Aizawl City is ________________ transport.

  Road

Question 2211 :
__________________ is the big concern for defining urban and rural areas.

  Activity/Employment

Question 2212 :
The towns which are notified under law by the concerned State/UT Government is called _____________________.

  Statutory Towns

Question 2213 :
Which of the planets is nearest to the earth? [Asstt Grade 1993]

  1. Jupiter
  2. Venus
  3. Mercury
  4. Mars
   Venus

Question 2214 :
The concept of Concentric Zone Theory was proposed in the year of ________________.

  1924

Question 2215 :
In Burges Concentric Zone Model, the Factory or shop workers are live in Zone number _________.

  III

Question 2216 :
Which one of the following pairs of straits and the countries they separate is wrongly matched?

  1. Gibraltar Strait - Spain and Morocco
  2. Bering Strait - Sumatra and Malaysia
  3. Magellan Strait - Chile and Tiera del Fuego
  4. Bass Strait - Australia and Tasmania
   Bering Strait - Sumatra and Malaysia

Question 2217 :
The rank size rule and attempted to express this relationship in mathematical terms is proposed by ______________________.

  G. K. Zipf

Question 2218 :
The minimum population that is required to bring about the provision of certain good or services is known as _____________________.

  Threshold

Question 2219 :
Christaller’s K=3 represents ________________ principle.

  Marketing

Question 2220 :
________________ zone is the farthest zone in Burgess Concentric Zone Theory.

  Commuters’
