Question 451 :
Which of the following is NOT a cash crop?

  1. Tobacco
  2. Cotton
  3. Coffee
  4. Bajara
   Bajara

Question 452 :
Railways are more in North Indian plains because

  1. the area is more productive
  2. it is densely populated
  3. the region offers least physical resistance
  4. All the above
   All the above

Question 453 :
The main hydroelectric power potential of the Indus river system lies in India and not in Pakistan because

  1. the need for hydroelectric power is greater in India than the Pakistan
  2. India has superior technology to develop hydroelectric power
  3. India has a greater labour force to develop hydroelectric power
  4. the mountain stages of these rivers lie in India
   the mountain stages of these rivers lie in India

Question 454 :

  1. United Kingdom
  2. Former USSR

Which of the following types of soils is found throughout India in all climatic zones?

  1. Punjab
  2. Bihar
  3. Arunachal Pradesh
  4. Chhattisgarh

Question 463 :
The finest natural harbour in India is

  1. Chennai
  2. Mumbai

  4. Cochin

Question 464 :
Which region gets first monsoon in summer?

  1. Himalayas
  2. Western Ghats
  3. Eastern Ghats
  4. Gangatic Plain

Question 465 :
Which one of the following is not conducive to the formation of deltas?

  1. Currents and Tides
  2. Calm winds
  3. Absence of large lakes along the course of the river
  4. Sheltered coastline

Question 466 :
In which part of India is Dogri spoken?

  1. West Bengal
  2. Assam
  3. Jammu and Kashmir
  4. Rajasthan
   Jammu and Kashmir

Question 467 :
Which of the following is the name of very violent and serious type of winds which bring a lot of disaster?

  1. Trade winds
  2. Hurricane
  3. Cirrus
  4. Stratus
   Hurricane

Question 468 :
The Coromandel Coast of India receives good rains from cyclonic storms in the months of

  1. February and March
  2. July and August
  3. November and December
  4. None of the above
   November and December

Question 469 :
Which of the following have almost the same point of beginning?

  1. Ganga and Indus
  2. Ganga and Brahmaputra
  3. Beas and Tapi
  4. Indus and Brahmaputra
   Indus and Brahmaputra

Question 470 :
The Indo-Gangetic Plains of India are fertile due to

  1. the heavy and timely rains and forests
  2. alluvial soils brought by the rivers from the mountains
  3. hard labour of the farmers over the generations
  4. better irrigation facilities
   alluvial soils brought by the rivers from the mountains

Question 471 :
Air travel has become very important for the north eastern part of India mainly because,

  1. the region is mountainous
  2. population density is low
  3. surface transport routes have to go around Bangladesh
  4. air transport is cheaper
   surface transport routes have to go around Bangladesh

Question 472 :
