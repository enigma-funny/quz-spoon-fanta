Question 31 :
Which of the following States does not share the drainage basin of the Krishna?

  1. Maharashtra
  2. Karnataka
  3. Andhra Pradesh
  4. Tamil Nadu
   Tamil Nadu

Question 32 :
Which of the following is a new bio-fertilizers?

  1. Green Blue Algae
  2. Nitrates
  3. Compost
  4. Farmyard manure
   Green Blue Algae

Question 33 :
Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) is in

  1. Mumbai
  2. Kolkata
  3. Bengaluru
  4. Chennai
   Bengaluru

Question 34 :
The water of the Luni river is

  1. Fresh
  2. Fresh in upper reaches and salty in the lower reaches
  3. Salty
  4. Fresh in rainy season and salty in other seasons
   Fresh in upper reaches and salty in the lower reaches

Question 35 :
The great one horned Indian Rhino is round in

  1. Corbett National Park
  2. Kanha National Park
  3. Kaziranga Game Sanctuary
  4. Periyar Game Sanctuary
   Kaziranga Game Sanctuary

Question 36 :
Which one of the following soil types of India is rendered infertile by the presence of excess iron?

  1. Desert sand
  2. Alluvial
  3. Podzolic
  4. Lateritic

Question 37 :

  1. Lunej

  2. Haematite

Question 40 :
Which of the following states is the Sugar Bowl of India?

  1. Uttar Pradesh
  2. Himachal Pradesh
  3. Tamil Nadu

Question 41 :
Ganga beyond Farakka when it enters Bangladesh is known as

  1. Padma
  2. Meghna
  3. Hugli

Question 42 :
The Indian States which have common borders with Pakistan are

  1. Gujarat, Himachal Pradesh, Haryana and Jammu - Kashmir
  2. Gujarat, Jammu - Kashmir, Punjab and Rajasthan,
  3. Jammu - Kashmir, Haryana, Rajasthan and Punjab
  4. Jammu - Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh, Punjab and Rajasthan

Question 43 :
Most rivers flowing west from the Western Ghats do not Corm deltas because of

  1. lack of eroded material
  2. the high gradient
  3. lack of vegetation free area
  4. low velocity
   the high gradient

Question 44 :
The system or shifting cultivation in the north eastern region of India is called

  1. Jhuming
  2. Bewar
  3. Taungya
  4. Ladang
   Jhuming

Question 45 :
________ State has the biggest area in India.

  1. Maharashtra
  2. Madhya Pradesh
  3. Rajasthan
  4. Uttar Pradesh
   Rajasthan

Question 46 :
What is Gomia in Jharkhand famous for?

  1. Coal fields
  2. Manganese mines
  3. Fertilizer plant
  4. Explosives factory
   Explosives factory

Question 47 :
What is the most important geographic use of the Himalayas to India?

  1. Prevention of invasions
  2. Valuable source of timber
  3. They prevent the monsoons from crossing to the north and also serve as a reservoir of water from which our perennial rivers flow
  4. They protect India from the icy cold north winds
   They prevent the monsoons from crossing to the north and also serve as a reservoir of water from which our perennial rivers flow

Question 48 :
The driest part in India is in

  1. Jaisalmer (Western Rajasthan)
  2. Bhatinda (Punjab)
  3. Darbhanga (Bihar)
  4. Gandhinagar (Gujarat)
   Jaisalmer (Western Rajasthan)

Question 49 :
HBJ pipe line is being laid for a distance of 1730 km in order to

  1. provide cooking gas for villages
  2. provide employment for rural population
  3. provide feedstock for six large fertilisers
  4. serve the power requirements of many states
   provide feedstock for six large fertilisers

Question 50 :
The Cudappah Ranges lie between

  1. Godavari and Palkonda Range
  2. Godavari and Jamshedpur
  3. Palar and Cauvery
  4. Satpura and Mohadeo-Maikal range
   Godavari and Palkonda Range

Question 51 :
The port commanding the largest hinterland in India is

  1. Mumbai
  2. Kandla
  3. Kolkata
  4. Chennai
   Kolkata

Question 52 :
The glacier of the Nubra Valley the longest one with a length of over 72 km is called the _______ glacier.

  1. Hispar
  2. Siachen
  3. Biafo
  4. Baltaro
   Siachen

Question 53 :
India has an endless growing season because

  1. it experiences the monsoonal type of climate
  2. the distribution of rainfall is uneven
  3. temperatures throughout the year are favourable for growth of plants
  4. its soil is very fertile the greater part being alluvial

Question 54 :
