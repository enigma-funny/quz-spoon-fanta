Question 841 :
The primate city of Mizoram is:

  1. Tlabung      
  2. Lunglei      
  3. Aizawl      
  4. Sairang
  Aizawl

Question 842 :
Pick out the correct answer:

  1. Census town must have at least more than 75% of the main working population engaged in non-agriculture activities        
  2. Census town must have at least more than 75% of the male main working population engaged in non-agriculture activities      
  3. both ( and ( are correct
  4. None of these
  Census town must have at least more than 75% of the male main working population engaged in non-agriculture activities

  2. Positivism   approach    
  3. Behavioral approach  
  4. Humanistic approach

Question 846 :
The concept of ‘Range of good or services’ is:

  1. the minimum population that is required to bring about the provision of certain good or services                                        
  2. the average maximum distance people will travel to purchase goods and services  
  3. a place which serves as a focal point for a number of other settlements which are dependent on it                                        
  4. None of the above
  the average maximum distance people will travel to purchase goods and services

Question 847 :

Question 848 :
Power minerals are

  1. manganese, platinum, tungsten
  2. mica, potash, sulphur
  3. coal, oil, natural gas
  4. lead, zinc, bauxite

Question 849 :

  1. Chhattisgarh
  2. Jharkhand
  3. Madhya Pradesh
  4. Orissa

Question 850 :

  1. Shopping facilities          
  2. Parking facilities  
  3. Driving facilities          
  4. None of them

Question 851 :
Rice in Myanmar is cultivated along the deltas of ______ river.

  1. Salween
  2. Menam
  3. Mekong
  4. Irrawaddy
   Irrawaddy

Question 852 :
Which of the following peninsular rivers is westward flowing?

  1. Mahanadi
  2. Godavari
  3. Tapti
  4. Cauvery
   Tapti

Question 853 :
Why does the mercury column in the barometer fall rapidly before a severe storm? It is due to

  1. increase in the humidity of air
  2. fall in the atmospheric pressure
  3. severe heat energy from the sun
  4. rise in the atmospheric pressure
   fall in the atmospheric pressure

Question 854 :
Consider the following problems being faced by the Indian Coal Industry : 1. Poor quality of coal and bottlenecks in the coal movement 2. Low utilisation capacity of washeries 3. Growing dependence on the import of coking coal 4. Administered prices. - Which of the above are correct? [BPSC 2011]

  1. II, III and IV
  2. I, II, III and IV
  3. I, III and IV
  4. I, II and III
   I, II, III and IV

Question 855 :
In the decreasing order of the length of the rivers, the correct sequence is : [C2003]

  1. Brahmaputra - Ganga - Godavari - Narmada
  2. Ganga - Godavari - Brahmaputra - Narmada
  3. Brahmaputra - Narmada - Godavari - Ganga
  4. Ganga - Brahmaputra - Godavari – Narmada
   Brahmaputra - Ganga - Godavari - Narmada

Question 856 :
Where is Pygmalion Point also known as Indira Point?

  1. Lakshadweep
  2. Andman and Nicobar Islands
  3. Rameswaram
  4. Point Calimere
   Andman and Nicobar Islands

Question 857 :
Consider the following: 1. Chilika Lake 2. Pulicat Lake 3. Vembanad Lake - Which of the above is/are lagoon(s)? [CDS 2005]

  1. 1 and 2
  2. 2 only
  3. 1 and 3
  4. 1, 2 and 3
   1, 2 and 3

Question 858 :
The continuous circulation of water among the hydrosphere, atmosphere and Lithosphere is known as

  1. Water circulation
  2. Hydrologic cycle
  3. Hydro-equilibrium
  4. Precipitation
   Hydrologic cycle

Question 859 :
European Turkey is separated from its Asian part by

  1. Straits of Bosporus
  2. Sea of Marmara
  3. Straits of Dardanelles
  4. All the above
   All the above

Question 860 :
On the Equator the day and night are equal

  1. on equinoxes
  2. on summer solstice
  3. on winter solstice
  4. throughout the year
   throughout the year

Question 861 :
Which one the following is a correct sequence of rivers in terms of their total basin area, in the descending order? [IAS 2003]

  1. Ganga, Indus, Brahmaputra, Godavari
  2. Indus, Ganga, Brahmaputra, Godavari
  3. Brahmaputra, Indus, Ganga, Godavari
  4. Indus, Ganga Godavari, Brahmaputra
   Brahmaputra, Indus, Ganga, Godavari

Question 862 :
The home of spices is

  1. Middle East
  2. The USA
  3. France
  4. India

Question 863 :
Which of the following states has the largest number of salt lakes in India?
