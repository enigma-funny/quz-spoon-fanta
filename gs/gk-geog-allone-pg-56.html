Question 1651 :
Which of the following is not a navigable river in India?

  1. Brahmaputra
  2. Godavari
  3. Narmada
  4. Chenab
   Chenab

Question 1652 :
Where will you expect to find bamboo growing

  1. At a great height from sea level
  2. At a medium height from sea level
  3. At a very low height from sea level
  4. There is no such condition; it can grow anywhere
   At a very low height from sea level

Question 1653 :
Blue Mountain (peak) is located in ________.

  1. Tamil Nadu
  2. Kerala
  3. Mizoram
  4. Kamataka
   Mizoram

Question 1654 :
Which of the Indian states has the maximum number of common borders with other Indian States?

  1. West Bengal
  2. Madhya Pradesh
  3. Uttar Pradesh
  4. Karnataka
   Madhya Pradesh

Question 1655 :
Which of the following south Indian rivers has the largest basin?

  1. Godavari
  2. Krishna
  3. Cauvery
  4. Mahanadi
   Godavari

Question 1656 :
After which one of the following tribes of India has a supercontinent of ancient geological history of the world been named?

  1. Santhals
  2. Khonds
  3. Bhils
  4. Gonds
   Gonds

Question 1657 :
Ganges, Brahmaputra, Mahanadi, Cauvery, Narmada, Godavari and Krishna are extensive areas of

  1. Inland fisheries
  2. Estuarine fisheries
  3. Coastal fisheries
  4. Deep sea fisheries
   Inland fisheries

Question 1658 :
The total length of the river Ganga is about

  1. 3000 km
  2. 3425 km
  3. 2500 km
  4. 2750 km
   2500 km

Question 1659 :
In an area with more than 200 cm annual rainfall and slopy hills, which of the following crops can be cultivated best?

  1. Tea
  2. Jute
  3. Tobacco
  4. Cotton
   Tea

Question 1660 :
Which type of soil retains maximum water?

  1. Sandy soil
  2. Clayey soil
  3. Loamy soil
  4. Rocky soil
   Clayey soil

Question 1661 :
What is the main attraction of Kaziranga Sanctuary located in Assam?

  1. Tiger
  2. Spotted deer
  3. Nilgai
  4. One horned Rhinoceros
   One horned Rhinoceros

Question 1662 :
_________ is known as the Queen of the Arabian Sea.

  1. Cochin
  2. Mumbai
  3. Mangalore
  4. Surat
   Cochin

Question 1663 :
_______ is not situated on the banks of the Ganga.

  1. Agra
  2. Kanpur
  3. Allahabad
  4. Varanasi
   Agra

Question 1664 :
Which of the following is not primarily a rabi crop in India?

  1. Wheat
  2. Gram
  3. Jute
  4. Linseed
   Jute

Question 1665 :
The western disturbances which cause winter rain in India originate in

  1. Pakistan
  2. Bay of Bengal
  3. West Asia
  4. Himalayas
   West Asia

Question 1666 :
The maximum percentage of the tribal population in India consists of

  1. Santhals
  2. Bhils
  3. Mundas
  4. Nagas
   Santhals

Question 1667 :
HMT industries are set up at

  1. Kalamassery
  2. Hyderabad and Bengaluru
  3. Pinjore and Ajmer
  4. All of the above
   All of the above

Question 1668 :
In which of the following States is Sambhar lake located?

  1. Madhya Pradesh
  2. Maharashtra
  3. Gujarat
  4. Rajasthan
   Rajasthan

Question 1669 :
With which one of the following industries is Coimbatore associated?

  1. Gold
  2. Gujarat
  3. Cotton Textile
  4. Sericulture
   Cotton Textile

Question 1670 :
In which of the following areas is Konkani spoken as the local language?

  1. Kerala and Karnataka
  2. Maharashtra and Goa
  3. Odisha and West Bengal
  4. Andhra Pradesh
   Maharashtra and Goa

Question 1671 :
India is the largest tea producing country in the world. It is grown in which of the following states mainly?

  1. Punjab
  2. Haryana
  3. Bihar
  4. Assam
   Assam

Question 1672 :
The Indian city famous for glass bangle industry is

  1. Lucknow
  2. Sikandrabad
  3. Firozabad
  4. Agra
   Firozabad

Question 1673 :
The river which rises in the Kamarpet hill in Chhotanagpur Plateau of Bihar and called The River of Sorrow is

  1. Mahanadi
  2. Damodar
  3. Krishna
  4. Godavari
   Damodar

Question 1674 :
The Matatilla Project is

  1. executed by Uttar Pradesh
  2. executed by Madhya Pradesh
  3. a joint venture by Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh
  4. a joint venture by Uttar Pradesh and Bihar
   a joint venture by Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh

Question 1675 :
The only State which produces saffron in the Indian Union is

  1. Assam
  2. Rajasthan
  3. Jammu and Kashmir
  4. Sikkim
   Jammu and Kashmir

Question 1676 :
Tanks are manufactured at

  1. Kanpur
  2. Chittaranjan
  3. Perambur
  4. Avadi
   Avadi

Question 1677 :
The Mountain Peak located where the Tsangpo-Brahmaputra river takes a hairpin bend from West-East trend to NorthSouth trend is

  1. Saramati
  2. Shillong
  3. Namcha Barwa
  4. Karbi
   Namcha Barwa

Question 1678 :
Which of the following group of rivers in India are likely to show the least erosional activity?

  1. Himalayan rivers
  2. Those rising in the Great Plain of India
  3. The thin streams flowing into the Arabian Sea
  4. The eastward flowing Peninsular rivers
   The eastward flowing Peninsular rivers

Question 1679 :
Where was India's rocket launching station set up?

  1. Sriharikota
  2. Arvi
  3. Thumba
  4. Port Blair
   Thumba

Question 1680 :
Pampero' is :

  1. a cold wind which prevails in Argentina and Uruguay
  2. a low pressure area in Equator
  3. a moisture laden wind which brings rainfall to the west coast of Panama
  4. a high pressure area which develops first and then leads to the formation of an anticyclone
   a cold wind which prevails in Argentina and Uruguay
