Question 121 :
The amount of actual water vapour of air is known as: [NDA 1992]

  1. absolute humidity
  2. dew point
  3. relative humidity
  4. condensation point
   absolute humidity

Question 122 :

Its density decreases with increasing height from the surface of the earth. 3. Its characteristic composition is same throughout its height. 4. Amount of pressure it exerts on earth's surface at sea level is called one atm sphere. Which of these statements are correct? [CDS 2001]

  1. 1 and 4
  2. 2 and 4
  3. 2, 3 and 4
  4. 1, 2 and 4

Question 123 :

Question 126 :
Which one of the following conditions is NOT necessary for precipitation to occur? [NDA 2002]

  1. The air must be warm
  2. The air must be saturated
  3. The air must be cooled below its dew point

Question 127 :

Question 128 :

Question 129 :
A temperature inversion is most likely to occur under which of the following conditions? [IAS 2004]

  1. Mountain top in late evening
  2. Windy but cloudy night
  3. Calm, cloudy and humid night

Question 130 :
Which one of the following characteristics is NOT associated with the tropical cyclones? [NDA 2002]

  1. Before the cyclone arrives the air becomes very still and temperature and humidity are high
  2. As the front of the vertex arrives, gusty winds develop and thick clouds appear
  3. As the cold front passes, the weather changes very rapidly
  4. Calm conditions return when the eye of the cyclone arrives
   Calm conditions return when the eye of the cyclone arrives

Question 131 :
Which one among the following statements relating to a cyclone is not correct? [Asstt Commdt 2011]

  1. It is a wind circulation system with a low pressure at the centre
  2. It brings about sudden change In weather condition in an area
  3. Wind movement towards the low pressure centre of a cyclone is clockwise in the northern hemisphere
  4. It is known as hurricane in West Indies
   Wind movement towards the low pressure centre of a cyclone is clockwise in the northern hemisphere

Question 135 :
