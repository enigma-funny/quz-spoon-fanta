Question 181 :
The Factory Act of 1881 was passed with a view to—

  1. Prohibit the employment of children below the age of 7 in any factory
  2. Allow the workers to trade unions
  3. Fix the wages of industrial workers
  4. Reduce the working hours for women employees
   Prohibit the employment of children below the age of 7 in any factory

Question 182 :
Svayamvara was a special form of—

  1. Jahandar Shah
  2. Shah Jahan II
  3. Mohammad Shah

Question 188 :
Which of the following known as ‘Jnani Manav’ —

  1. During the later Vedic age women enjoyed the right to upanayana ceremony
  2. During the later Vedic age there was no polygamy among aryans
