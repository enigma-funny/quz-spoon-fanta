Question 1 :
Which of the following tributaries of the Ganga System flows northwards? [CDS 2008]

  1. Kosi
  2. Ghaghara
  3. Gandak
  4. Sone
   Sone

Question 2 :
Which of the following is false?

  1. Krishna serves Maharashtra and Andhra Pradesh
  2. Godavari serves Madhya Pradesh
  3. Mahanadi serves Bihar and Orissa
  4. Narmada serves Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh
   Godavari serves Madhya Pradesh

Question 3 :

  1. Tapti
  2. Narmada
  3. Mahanadi

   Mahanadi

  1. 1, 2 and 3
  2. 1 and 3
  3. 2, 3 and 4
  4. 2 and 3

Question 7 :
The Brahmaputra, Irrawady and Mekong rivers originate in Tibet and flow through narrow and parallel mountain ranges in their upper reaches: Of these rivers, Brahmaputra makes a U turn in its course to flow into India. This U turn is due to [CSAT 2011]

  1. Uplift of folded Himalayan series
  2. Syntaxial bending of geologically young Himalayas
  3. Geo-tectonic disturbance in the tertiary folded mountain chains
  4. Both (a) and (b) above
   Both (a) and (b) above

Question 8 :

Question 9 :
Which one of the following statements in not correct? [IAS 2006]

  1. Mahanadi River rises in Chattisgarh
  2. Godavari River rises in Maharashtra
  3. Cauvery River rises in Andhra Pradesh
  4. Tapti River rises in Madhya Pradesh

Question 10 :
Which of the following is the deepest landlocked protected port?

  2. Vishakhapatnam

Question 11 :
Which of the following are the features of the Himalayan rivers? 1. They are of youthful nature, forming deep gorges and performing intense erosional activity 2. They are free to form their courses and follow a dendritic pattern 3. They have very large basins

  1. I and II
  2. I and III
  3. II and III
  4. I, II and III
   I, II and III

Question 12 :
River Damodar is called 'Sorrow of Bengal' because it : [CPO SI 2003]

  1. gets flooded often causing havoc
  2. causes maximum soil erosion
  3. forms number of dangerous waterfalls
  4. is not a perennial river
   gets flooded often causing havoc

Question 15 :
