Question 61 :

  2. Sal
  3. Sandalwood

Question 62 :

Question 65 :
How do the Central Asian highlands affect the Indian climate?

  1. The low pressure in these areas intensifies the cold during the winter
  2. The atmospheric pressure in this region has an effect on the Indian atmosphere in summer
  3. Dry continental air is blowing towards India throughout
  4. The atmospheric pressure in this region has an impact on the onset of monsoon in India
   The atmospheric pressure in this region has an impact on the onset of monsoon in India
