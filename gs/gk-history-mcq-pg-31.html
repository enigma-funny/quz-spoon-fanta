Question 901 :
That Bhagavatism first grew up around the Yamuna in Mathura district is evident from the account of—

  1. Hinyana
  2. Mahayana
  3. Vajrayana
  4. Lamaism
   Vajrayana

Question 902 :
In the Mughal administration, Muhtasib was —

  1. ¼
  2. 1/3
  3. 1/11
  4. 1/6
   1/3

Question 903 :
Harihara and Bukka, the founders of the Vijayanagara kingdom shaped the course of their conduct on the advice of

  1. Madurai
  2. Hampi
  3. Dwarasamudra
  4. Warangal

Question 904 :
The first battle of the American War of Independence took place in the year

  1. 1776, 4 July
  2. 1778, 14 August
  3. 1776, 28 July
  4. 1789, 14 July

Question 906 :

Question 907 :

Question 908 :

  1. Nawab Alivardi Khan
  2. Nanda Kumar
  3. Siraj-ud-daulah
  4. Shuja-ud-daulah

Question 909 :
An area which was relatively neglected by the Hindus, but where Muslim writers showed their skill, was —

  1. Goddess Lakshmi on the obverse and his name in Nagari characters on the reverse
  2. Kalima on one side and his name on the other in Arabic characters
  3. His name on the obverse and name of mint and year of minting on the reverse
  4. Zodiac signs on the obverse and his name in persian characters on the reverse
   Goddess Lakshmi on the obverse and his name in Nagari characters on the reverse

Question 910 :
Abul Fazal refers in his writings to the —

  1. Sindh
  2. Gujarat
  3. Punjab
  4. Both (a) and (b)

Dhavanyaloka is —

  3. Rana Kumbha
  4. Bappa Raval
