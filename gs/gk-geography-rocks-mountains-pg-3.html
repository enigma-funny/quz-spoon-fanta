Question 61 :
Which one among the following is the largest temperate desert of the world? [NDA 2011]

  1. Patagonian desert
  2. Taklamakan desert
  3. Iranian desert
  4. Turkmen desert
   Patagonian desert

Question 62 :
Caldera' is a feature associated with: [IAS 1990]

  1. volcanoes
  2. earthquakes
  3. folding of rocks
  4. faulting of mountains
   volcanoes

Question 63 :
Which of the following features are product of vulcanicity? 1. Atoll 2. Caldera 3. Batholith 4. Geosyncline

  1. I, II and III
  2. I and IV
  3. II and III
  4. I, III and IV
   II and III

Question 64 :
Which of the following are true with regard to igneous rocks? 1. They are formed inside the earth, under great pressure 2. They do not occur in layer 3. They are mostly crystalline in nature 4. They contain fossils

  1. I, II and III
  2. II, III and IV
  3. II and III
  4. I and IV
   II, III and IV

Question 65 :
The transverse, longitudinal and surface waves in an earthquake originate from:

  1. the focus within the body of the earth
  2. the focus on the surface of the earth
  3. the epicentre on the surface of the earth
  4. the epicentre within the body of the earth
   the focus within the body of the earth

Question 66 :
Earthquakes and volcanoes occur mostly in:

  1. Plateau region
  2. Deep sea plains
  3. Coastal Region
  4. Folded and faulted region
   Folded and faulted region

Question 67 :
Which one among the following is the type of the Comoros Islands which lie in the Indian Ocean between Northern Madagascar and the African Coast? [CDS 2012]

  1. Volcanic
  2. Glacial eroded
  3. Eolian deposited
  4. Folded
   Volcanic

Question 68 :
Which of the following influence the erosion of soil by water? [Railways 1994] 1. Slope of the land 2. Management practice like terracing 3. Soil salinity

  1. 1, 2 and 3
  2. 1 and 2
  3. 2 and 3
  4. 1 and 3
   1 and 2

Question 69 :
Folding is the result of [BPSC 2011]

  1. epeirogenetic force
  2. Coriolis force
  3. orogenetic force

Question 70 :
Which of the following is not an agent which produces physical features ?

  1. Denudation
  2. Deposition
  3. Volcanic eruption
  4. Conduction
   Conduction

Question 71 :
Which of the following is a block mountain?

  1. Andes
  2. Alps
  3. Rockies
  4. Vosges

Question 72 :

  1. Biological weathering
  2. Chemical weathering
  3. Degradational weathering

Question 73 :
The instrument, used for recording earthquake waves is :

  1. barograph
  2. hydrograph

Question 74 :
Which of the following forms of coal is the oldest? [IES 1991]

  1. Peat
  2. Lignite
  3. Bituminous
  4. Anthracite

Question 75 :
Consider the following statements: 1. The acid lava usually melts at a relatively higher temperature as compared to basic (basaltic) lava. 2. The acid lava solidifies into glass-like sheets and the basaltic lava solidifies into rough surfaces. - Which of the statements given above is/are correct? [IAS 2007]

  1. 1 only
  2. 2 only
  3. Both 1 and 2
  4. Neither 1 nor 2
   Both 1 and 2

Question 76 :
The largest volcano crater in the world is :

  1. Vesuvius (Italy)
  2. Toba (Indonesia)
  3. Etna (Sicily)
  4. Stromboli (Sicily)
   Toba (Indonesia)

Question 77 :
Which of the following are formed due to collision of continental plates? [IAS 2004] 1. The Alps 2. The Himalayas 3. The Rockies 4. The Caucases mountains - Select the correct answer using the codes given below:

  1. 1, 2 and 3
  2. 2, 3 and 4
  3. 1, 2 and 4
  4. 1, 3 and 4
   1, 2 and 3

Question 78 :
Which of the following features is the product of vulcanicity ? [NDA 2008]

  1. Fold Mountain
  2. Escarpment
  3. Geosynclines
  4. Caldera

Question 79 :
The well-defined zones of seismic activity in the world where the largest number of earthquakes originates is :

  1. Mediterranean Zone
  2. Circum Pacific Belt
  3. Mid oceanic Rift Valley Zone
  4. African Rift Valley Zone
   Circum Pacific Belt

Question 80 :
The Himalayas were formed due to :

  1. internal pressure of two large land forms
  2. faulting of the earth's crust
  3. folding of the earth's crust
  4. folding of the geo-syncline named Tethys
   folding of the geo-syncline named Tethys

Question 81 :
Choose the correct statement about Sedimentary rocks: [Teachers' Exam 1994]

  1. These rocks cannot be formed under water
  2. These rocks are crystalline
  3. The structure of these rocks is contingent on heat and pressure
  4. These rocks have been deposited in layers
   These rocks have been deposited in layers

Question 82 :
Igneous rocks are said to be acidic when they contain high proportion of:

  1. iron
  2. magnesium
  3. silica

Question 83 :
The intensity of earthquakes is measured on : [NDA 1992]

Question 84 :
