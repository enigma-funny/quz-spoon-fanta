Question 331 :
Which of the following south Indian rivers has the largest basin?

  1. Godavari
  2. Krishna
  3. Cauvery
  4. Mahanadi
   Godavari

Question 332 :
Which multipurpose project was taken up first of all after independence?

  1. Bhakra Nangal Project
  2. Damodar Valley Project
  3. Kosi Project
  4. None of these
   Damodar Valley Project

Question 333 :
Narmada originates from its source situated in the State of

  1. Maharashtra
  2. Uttar Pradesh
  3. Madhya Pradesh
  4. Andhra Pradesh
   Madhya Pradesh

Question 334 :
Which of the following is incorrect?

  1. Red soils are rich in iron
  2. Black soils are rich in phosphorus nitrogen and organic matter
  3. Alluvial soils are rich in potash but poor in phosphorus
  4. Red soils are suitable for cultivation of pulses and coarse grains
   Black soils are rich in phosphorus nitrogen and organic matter

Passenger and merchant ships are constructed at

Question 342 :
The Mountain Peak located where the Tsangpo-Brahmaputra river takes a hairpin bend from West-East trend to NorthSouth trend is

Question 343 :
Which of the following group of rivers in India are likely to show the least erosional activity?

  1. Himalayan rivers
  2. Those rising in the Great Plain of India
  3. The thin streams flowing into the Arabian Sea
  4. The eastward flowing Peninsular rivers
   The eastward flowing Peninsular rivers

Question 344 :

  2. Bhima and Kalinadi
  3. Varada and Malaprabha
  4. Gangawali and Savitri
   Zuari and Mandovi

Question 353 :
