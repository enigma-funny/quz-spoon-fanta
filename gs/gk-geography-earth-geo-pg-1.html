Question 1 :
A one-day Cricket match between India and England starts at 10.00 A.M. in London. The direct telecast of the match will begin in India at: [IFS 1993, CDS 1992]

  1. 3.30 A.M.
  2. 8.30 A.M.
  3. 3.30 P.M.
  4. 8.30 P.M.
   3.30 P.M.

Question 2 :
Variation in duration of day and night is due to: 1. rotation of earth on its axis 2. revolution of earth around the sun 3. inclination of earth at an angle of

  1. 1and 2
  2. 2 and 3
  3. 1 and 3
  4. 1, 2 and 3
   2 and 3

Question 3 :
Latitude of a point on the earth is measured by the distance in: [Asstt Grade 1990]

  1. kilometers from the poles
  2. kilometers from the equator
  3. angles from the poles
  4. angles from the equator

  1. I, III and IV
  2. II and IV
  3. III and IV
  4. II, III and IV

Question 5 :

  1. 6 km
  2. 1000 km
  3. 3600 km

Question 6 :
Which of the following is not an effect produced by rotation of earth?

  1. It causes days and nights
  2. All heavenly bodies like sun, moon, planets appear to move from East to West
  3. Winds change their directions
  4. Poles have days and nights of 6 months duration
   Poles have days and nights of 6 months duration

Question 7 :
If news is broadcast from London at 10:30 AM, at what time it will be heard at Baghdad (45°E)? [CDS 2012]

  1. 7:30 AM
  2. 9:00 AM
  3. 1:30 PM
  4. 12:00 Noon

Question 8 :
The South Pole experiences continuous light at:

  1. summer solstice
  2. winter solstice
  3. vernal equinox
  4. no time
   winter solstice

Question 9 :
The seasonal contrasts are maximum in:

  1. low latitudes
  2. mid-latitudes
  3. sub tropics
  4. high latitudes
   mid-latitudes

Question 10 :
The Solar eclipse achieves totality only in limited geographical regions because: [IAS 1993]

  1. the trajectories of the Earth around the Sun and the Moon around the Earth are not perfect circles
  2. the size of the shadow of the Moon on the Earth is small compared to the cross-section of the Earth
  3. sun rays can reach most of the peripheral regions of the shadow of the moon due to atmospheric refraction
  4. the Earth is not a smooth flat surface, but has elevations and depressions
   the size of the shadow of the Moon on the Earth is small compared to the cross-section of the Earth

  2. Asia

Question 16 :
Which of the following are true regarding equinoxes? [CDS 1993] - 1. They occur on March 21 st and September 23rd - 2. They depict equal days and equal. nights on March 21 st and September 23rd on all places of the earth - 3. All places on the earth have equal temperature during equinoxes

  1. 1 and 2 only
  2. 2 and 3 only
  3. 1 and 3 only
  4. 1, 2 and 3

Question 17 :
Tides are complied and they vary from place to place because of: [IAS 2003]

  1. the movement of Moon in relation to Earth
  2. Uneven distribution of water over the globe
  3. irregularities in the configuration of oceans
  4. all of the above
   all of the above

Question 18 :
Latitudes include 181 lines while longitudes are 360 lines because:

  1. longitude is divided from prime meridian while latitude is from equator
  2. longitude is divided from equator while latitude is from prime meridian
  3. longitude is a line that connects North Pole to South Pole and latitude is divided from east to west
  4. None of these
   longitude is divided from prime meridian while latitude is from equator

Question 19 :
Which of the following is the best definition of 'Tides' ? [SBI PO 1991]

  1. A powerful movement of the ocean which proceed constantly in a definite direction
  2. A great circulatory movement of sea water caused by difference in density of sea water
  3. A periodic rise and fall of sea water caused by the gravitational force of moon and sun on earth
  4. A wind and pressure system having a low pressure in centre and circular wind motion
   A periodic rise and fall of sea water caused by the gravitational force of moon and sun on earth

Question 22 :
The mapping of which of the following is most difficult ?

  1. Mountains
  2. Plateaus and Plains
  3. Interior of the Earth

Question 23 :
Lunar eclipse does not occur every month because:

  1. the sun's orbit is not always in the same plane as the moon
  2. the moon revolves at a slower speed than the earth
