Question 451 :
Which of the following are reasons for the identification of the reflected in the later Vedic texts with the painted grey ware culture? Select the correct answer (1) Material culture of the two corresponds (2) Geographical distribution of the two corresponds (3) Chronological span of the two corresponds (4) Both evolved locally

  1. The excavations at Harappa are attributed to R.B. Daya Ram Sahni
  2. The Assembly Hall was discovered at Mohenjo-daro
  3. The open courtyard was the basic feature of house planning
