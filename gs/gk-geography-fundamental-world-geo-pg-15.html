Question 421 :
A fumarole is

  1. a small crack on the side of volcano ejecting gas and steam
  2. a river truncated by river piracy
  3. a rock projecting through ice cap
  4. an underground cavern
   a small crack on the side of volcano ejecting gas and steam

Question 422 :
The world's largest inland sea is ______.

  1. the Black Sea
  2. the Dead Sea
  3. the Caspian Sea
  4. the Aral Sea
   the Caspian Sea

Question 423 :
What is a shaft mine?

  1. A query
  2. An open pit mine
  3. A mine dug deep into the earth
  4. A mine for descending into which a shaft is generally used

Question 424 :
Assemblage or ideas, beliefs, skills, institutions is represented by

  1. civilization

Question 436 :

Question 437 :

  1. Cape of Good Hope route
  2. Seuz Canal
  3. Panama Canal
  4. North Atlantic route
   Seuz Canal

Question 438 :
Which of the following natural regions is inhabited by the Eskimos of Canada and Alaska, Lappas of Finland and the Samoyeds and Yakuts of Siberia?

  1. High Mountain region
  2. Polar Tundra
  3. The Taiga region
  4. Cool (Mid-Latitude) West margin region
   Polar Tundra

Question 439 :
Why does the mercury column in the barometer fall rapidly before a severe storm? It is due to

  1. increase in the humidity of air
  2. fall in the atmospheric pressure
  3. severe heat energy from the sun
  4. rise in the atmospheric pressure
   fall in the atmospheric pressure

Question 440 :
