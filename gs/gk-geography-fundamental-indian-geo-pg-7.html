  1. Assam
  2. Arunachal Pradesh
  3. Mizoram
  4. Meghalaya
   Assam

  1. trees like rubber

Question 191 :

Question 192 :
Which of the following is not renowned for the manufacture of silk?

  1. Bhagalpur (Bihar)
  2. Srinagar (Jammu and Kashmir)
  3. Moradabad (Uttar Pradesh)
  4. Mysore (Karnataka)

Question 193 :

  1. Aluminium
  2. Calcium
  3. Nitrogen
  4. Phosphorus
   Calcium

Question 194 :
Which one of the following pairs is not correct?

  1. New Moore Island - India
  2. Hurricane - China Sea
  3. Doldrums - The equator
  4. Trade winds - Winds which blow towards the equator
   Hurricane - China Sea

Question 195 :
The first marine sanctuary in India, having within its bounds coral reefs, mollusca, dolphins, tortoises and various kinds of sea birds, has been established in

  1. Sundarbans
  2. Chilka Lake
  3. Gulf of Kachchh
  4. Lakshadweep
   Chilka Lake

Question 196 :
The weather office predicts depression over a certain area.

  1. cloudy skies
  2. atmospheric pressure in that area is lower than that in the surrounding areas
  3. heavy weather causing a feeling of depression
  4. low atmospheric pressure over a large area
