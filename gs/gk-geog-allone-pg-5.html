Question 121 :
When dew point is reached it is said that at that temperature

  1. the atmosphere is said to be saturated with water vapour
  2. the relative humidity of the atmosphere is 100%
  3. the atmosphere can hold no more water vapour
  4. All the above
   All the above

Question 122 :

  1. The expansion of poorly planned, low-density, auto-dependent development, which spreads out over large amounts of land                          
  2. The study of the life of the city                                
  3. The movement of people out of cities to the surrounding areas
