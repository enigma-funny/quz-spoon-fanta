Question 1802 :

  1. B. G. Tilak
  2. Gopal Krishna Gokhale
  3. Lala Lajpat Rai
  4. Raja Ram Mohan Roy

Question 1803 :

  1. Alauddin Khalji
  2. Balban
  3. Feroze Shah Tughlaq
  4. Sikandar Lodi

Question 1804 :
There is evidence of the proto-Australoid, the mediterranean, alpine and Mongoloid in the skeletal remains at—

  1. 6000-3000 B.C.
  2. 4000—3000 B.C.
  3. 5000—3000 B.C.
  4. 3000-1000 B.C.

Question 1805 :
Which of the following is/are true regarding Buddhism? 1) It did not reject Varna - Jati 2) It challenged highest social ranking of Brahmana Varna. 3) It regarded certain crafts as low . Choose the correct answer from the codes given below—

  1. Right speech, right action, right exertion, right mindedness
  2. Right meditation, right resolution, right view - right means livelihood
  3. Dukham (Misery) Dukh samudaya (thirst, attachment etc.) Nirodha (end of worldly existence) - Marga (Patha)
  4. All of above
   Dukham (Misery) Dukh samudaya (thirst, attachment etc.) Nirodha (end of worldly existence) - Marga (Patha)

Question 1809 :
Which one of the following statement is correct?

  1. The Indian Independence Act, 1947 gave unlimited powers to the Constitution Assembly of each Dominion to frame and adopt any Constitution and to repeal any act of the British Parliament
  2. The Indian Independence Act, 1947 came into force on the 15th August, 1947
  3. The Indian Independence Act, 1947 provided that the district of Silhet in Assam would form part of India
  4. The Indian Independence Act, 1947 made it mandatory that the Constituent Assemblies of India and Pakish should consist of members directly exected by the people

Question 1816 :
