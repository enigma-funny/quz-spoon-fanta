Question 91 :
The jet aircrafts fly very easily and smoothly in the lower stratosphere. What could be the appropriate explanation? 1. There are no clouds or water vapour in the lower stratosphere. 2. There are no vertical winds in the lower stratosphere. Which of the statements given above is/are correct in this context? [CSAT 2011]

  1. 1 only
  2. 2 only
  3. Both 1 and 2
  4. Neither 1 nor 2
   Both 1 and 2

Question 92 :
Which of the following is a cold wind?

  1. Buran
  2. Foehn
  3. Chinook
  4. Sirocco
   Buran

Question 93 :
In the earth's atmosphere, the atmospheric pressure: [NDA 1991]

  1. increases with height
  2. decreases with height
  3. remains constant with height
  4. first increases and then decreases with height
   decreases with height

Question 94 :
The cauliflower shape is associated with which of the following cloud formations?

  1. Stratus
  2. Cumulus
  3. Nimbus
  4. Cirrus
   Cumulus

Question 95 :
The plane of contact of the troposphere and stratosphere is known as :

  1. mesosphere
  2. ionosphere
  3. stratopause
  4. tropopause
   tropopause

Question 96 :
Mist is a result of which one of the following? [CDS 2008]

  1. Condensation
  2. Evaporation
  3. Sublimation
  4. Saturation
   Condensation

Question 97 :
Winds and air currents differ in the aspect that:

  1. air currents blow much faster than winds
  2. air currents are always moisture laden while the winds are usually dry
  3. the winds blow on lands while air currents blow over seas and oceans
  4. winds are horizontal movements of air while air currents are the vertical movements of air
   winds are horizontal movements of air while air currents are the vertical movements of air

Question 98 :
Consider the following statements: [Asstt Comm 2008] 1. Rainfall in the doldrums is of convectional nature. 2. Convectional rainfall is highly localized and is associated with minimum cloudiness. Which of the statements given above is/are correct?

  1. 1 only
  2. Both 1 and 2
  3. 2 only
  4. Neither 1 nor 2
   1 only

Question 99 :
The dry wind 'Santa Ana' blows in :

  1. Siberia
  2. Argentina
  3. Switzerland
  4. California

Question 100 :
Atmospheric humidity is measured by: [NDA 1994]

  1. Psychrometer
  2. Anemometer
  3. Lysimeter
  4. Hydrometer
   Psychrometer

Question 101 :
The instrument used for finding out wind-direction is :

  1. anemometer
  2. wind vane
  3. wind gauze
  4. hygrometer
   wind vane

Question 102 :
Atmospheric pressure exerted on earth is due to:

  1. Gravitational pull of the earth
  2. Rotation of earth
  3. Revolution of earth
  4. Uneven heating of earth
   Gravitational pull of the earth

Question 103 :
Choose the incorrect statement:

  1. Capacity of air to hold moisture decreases with a rise in temperature of the air
  2. The air pressure decreases with an increase in temperature
  3. Absolute humidity is expressed in grams per cubic metre of air
  4. With an ascent of every 165 metres, the temperature is reduced by 1°C
   Absolute humidity is expressed in grams per cubic metre of air

Question 104 :
Which one among the following forces is most powerful in determining movement of wind including its velocity ? [Asstt Commdt 2011]

  1. Gravitational force
  2. Centrifugal force
  3. Frictional force
  4. Pressure gradient force
   Pressure gradient force

Question 105 :
Because of which one of the following factors, clouds do not precipitate in deserts? [CDS 2009]

  1. Low pressure
  2. Low humidity
  3. High wind velocity
  4. High temperature
   Low humidity

Question 106 :
The Trade winds are caused by:

  1. rotation of the earth
  2. revolution of the earth
  3. inflowing movement of air along the earth's surface towards the equator
  4. none of these
   inflowing movement of air along the earth's surface towards the equator

In the humid subtropical climate of Kyushu island of Japan, the rainfall for the year almost totals that of the rainfall in tropical rainforest. Which of the statements given above is/are correct? [IAS 2007]

  1. 1only
  2. 2 only
  3. Both 1 and 2
  4. Neither 1 nor 2
   Both 1 and 2

Question 108 :
Wind in the Southern hemisphere is deflected towards left due to : [CDS 2001]

  1. temperature variations
  2. rotation of the earth
  3. difference in pressure
  4. difference in water content
   rotation of the earth

Question 109 :
Which of the following places are favourably influenced by the Trade winds? 1. West Indies 2. Brazil 3. Mexico Medagascar

  1. I and II
  2. I, II, III and IV
  3. III and IV
  4. II and IV

Question 110 :
Which one of the following statements is correct? [IAS 2004]

  1. Cirrus clouds are composed of ice crystals
  2. Cirrus clouds exhibit a flat base and have the appearance of rising domes
  3. Cumulus clouds are white and thin, and form delicate patches and give a fibrous and feathery appearance
  4. Cumulus clouds are classified as high clouds
   Cirrus clouds are composed of ice crystals

Question 111 :
‘Temperate lows’ are:

  1. low pressure areas in cyclones
  2. equatorial low pressure belt
  3. polar regions which have very low temperatures
  4. sub-polar low pressure belts

Question 112 :
When can one record the lowest temperature of air?

  1. Just before sunrise
  2. At midnight
  3. At 3 a.m.

Question 113 :
In the atmosphere of the primitive earth there was no :

The phenomenon of 'trade winds' takes place due to [NDA 2011]

Question 115 :
Increase in carbon dioxide in atmosphere causes: [Railways 1994]

  1. rise in earth temperature

Question 116 :
Condensation is a process which occurs when [IAS 2009]

  2. 2 only
  3. Both 1 and 2
  4. Neither 1 nor 2

  3. South-western

Question 119 :
What causes snowfall?

  1. When condensation takes place below 0° C and precipitation occurs in the form of flakes
  2. When rain freezes into snow-flakes while passing through the atmosphere
  3. When condensation takes place in form of fog and precipitation occurs as flakes
  4. When the air temperature is much lower than surface temperature
   When condensation takes place below 0° C and precipitation occurs in the form of flakes
