Question 1291 :
The only port in erstwhile Soviet Union that is ice tree throughout the year is

  1. Leningrad
  2. Odessa
  3. Murmansk
  4. Vladivostok
   Murmansk

Question 1292 :
Which one among the following statements relating to a cyclone is not correct? [Asstt Commdt 2011]

  1. It is a wind circulation system with a low pressure at the centre
  2. It brings about sudden change In weather condition in an area
  3. Wind movement towards the low pressure centre of a cyclone is clockwise in the northern hemisphere
  4. It is known as hurricane in West Indies
   Wind movement towards the low pressure centre of a cyclone is clockwise in the northern hemisphere

  1. Superior
  2. Michigan

Question 1301 :
Plains formed by the deposits brought by glacier are

  1. lacustrine plains
  2. flood plains
  3. drift plains
  4. coastal plains

Question 1302 :
Which one of the following towns is not situated along the Grand Trunk Road?

  1. Allahabad
  2. Lucknow
  3. Agra
  4. Kanpur
   Lucknow

Question 1303 :
A strait is

  1. a narrow stretch of sea connecting two extensive areas of sea
  2. a narrow strip of land joining two large land-masses
  3. a wide indentation into the land formed by a sea or a lake
  4. a narrow headland
   a narrow stretch of sea connecting two extensive areas of sea

Question 1304 :

  1. Singapore
  2. Bangkok

Question 1305 :

  1. Northern Atlantic
  2. South Atlantic

Question 1306 :
What is true with regard to Jet Streams? 1. High velocity winds 2. Blow from west to east 3. Blow in upper troposphere near the tropopause 4. Blow in the mesosphere

  1. 2 and 3
  2. 2 and 4
  3. 1, 2 and 3
  4. 1, 2 and 4

Question 1307 :
