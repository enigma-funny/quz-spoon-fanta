Question 211 :

  1. 1 only
  2. 2 and 3
  3. 1 and 3
  4. 3 only

Question 216 :
In which of the following battles with the Sikhs did the Partisans of the holy war suffer a terrible defeat in which Sayyaid Ahamed lost his life?

  1. Paleolithic and Mesolithic people practised painting.
  2. Pre-historic art appears at several places, but Bhimbetka is the most striking site with 500 painted rock shelters.

Question 218 :
Vikrama era started from:

  1. According to the Vedic thinkers, monarch was the ultimate source of law
  2. According to the Vedic thinkers, People were the ultimate source of law
  3. According to the Vedic thinkers, the nature was the ultimate source of law
  4. According to the Vedic thinkers, Dharma which sustained the world as the ultimate source of law
