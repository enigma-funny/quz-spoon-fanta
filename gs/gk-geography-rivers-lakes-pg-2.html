Question 31 :
Which of the following peninsular rivers is westward flowing?

  1. Mahanadi
  2. Godavari
  3. Tapti
  4. Cauvery

Question 32 :

  1. Damodar
  2. Hooghly
  3. Godavari
  4. Swarnarekha

  2. Dhansiri

Question 35 :

  1. Mahanadi
  2. Godavari
  3. Cauvery
  4. Krishna

Question 36 :
Which of the following is true with regard to the, characteristics of the Himalayan rivers? 1. Many of them have their sources in the Inner Himalayas 2. They have a perennial flow 3. They have a great capacity for erosion 4. They do not form gorges

  1. I and II
  2. I, II and III
  3. III and IV
  4. II and IV
   I, II and III

Question 37 :
Consider the following rivers: 1. Ganga 2. Brahmaputra 3. Godavari 4. Krishna - Which of these rivers build a common delta? [CDS 2001]

  1. 1 and 4
  2. 1 and 3
  3. 1 and 2
  4. 3 and 4
   1 and 2

  1. Bharatpur
  2. Jaipur
  3. Jodhpur

  2. Ganga, Yamuna, Godavari
  3. Ganga, Yamuna, Brahmaputra

Question 43 :
Which of the following lakes were formed by volcanic activities? [IAS 2003] (A) Lonar lake (B) Bhim Tal of Kumaun (C) Toba lake (D) Keloed lake

  1. A and C
  2. A and D
  3. A, C and D

Question 44 :

  2. 1, 3 and 4
  3. 2 and 4
  4. 1, 2, 3 and 4

Question 46 :

  2. 3, 4, 1, 2
  3. 4, 3, 2, 1

Question 48 :

Question 49 :

  2. 2, 1, 4, 3

Question 50 :
River Godavari flows through: [NDA 1994]

  3. Maharashtra, Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh

Question 51 :

Question 52 :

Question 53 :
Canal irrigation is most important in the Northern Plains of India because:
