Question 1777 :

Question 1778 :
Clive introduced dual Govemm1t in Bengal because—

  1. He was afraid of hostile reaction from the people of Bengal
  2. The authorities of the East India Company in England did not allow him to establishment of de jure authority of the East India Company in Bengal
  3. The Mughal emperor was opposed to the establishment of de jure authority of the East India Company in Bengal
  4. He wanted to avoid the responsibility arising out of the establishment of de jure authority of the East India Company in Bengal.
   He wanted to avoid the responsibility arising out of the establishment of de jure authority of the East India Company in Bengal.

Question 1785 :
In Mughal period Zamindars were—

  1. Widow marriage was allowed among the Rigvedic Aryans

  3. Sati custom was common among the Rigvedic Aryans

Question 1795 :
Religion-wise the sculptures found at Kankali Tila in Mathura are—

  1. It has an umbrella at the top
  2. It has a sanctum sanctorum
  3. It has a circumambulatory path (Pradakshinapatha)

Question 1796 :
Akbar’s buildings at Fatehpur Sikri are famous for—

  1. Synthesis of trabeate and accurate styles
  2. Construction of the first bulbous dome in India
  3. The use of marble on an extensive scale
  4. Frequent employment of pietra dura
