Question 391 :
The National Geophysical Research Institute is located in

  1. Pilani
  2. Goa
  3. Lucknow
  4. Hyderabad
   Hyderabad

Question 392 :
In ancient times the name Ratnakar denote

  1. Arabian Sea
  2. Bay of Bengal
  3. Indian Ocean
  4. Confluence of Ganga, Yamuna and Saraswati
   Arabian Sea

Question 393 :
Hazaribagh district in Jharkhand has rich deposits of

  1. gold
  2. mica
  3. gypsum
  4. lead
   mica

Question 394 :
Which of the following states does not share the drainage basin of the Cauvery?

  1. Andhra Pradesh
  2. Karnataka
  3. Kerala
  4. Tamil Nadu
   Andhra Pradesh

Question 395 :
The National Atlas and Thematic Mapping Organisation (NATMO) is located at

  1. Kolkata
  2. Lucknow
  3. Dehradun
  4. New Delhi
   Kolkata

Question 396 :
What is the most important characteristic of the islands (Indian) located in the Arabian Sea?

  1. They are all very small in size
  2. They are all of coral origin
  3. They have a very dry climate
  4. They are extended parts of the mainland
   They are all of coral origin

Question 397 :

  2. Tungabhadra
  3. Krishna
  4. Godavari

Question 407 :
The Sharavati Project is a/an

  1. thermal project

Question 408 :
The Gobindsagar Reservoir is in

  3. Ranjit Sagar Dam
  4. Rajasthan Canal
   Bhakra Dam

Question 409 :
Why is the Delta of the Indus small compared to the Delta of the Ganga?

  1. The Indus traverses a shorter distance than the Ganga
  2. The Indus is not as broad as the Ganga
  3. The path of the Indus is through arid area, whereas the Ganga collects more water alone its path of the sea
  4. None of the above
   The path of the Indus is through arid area, whereas the Ganga collects more water alone its path of the sea

Question 410 :
Which of the following places does not lie on the banks of Godavari?

  1. Nanded
  2. Bhadrachalam
  3. Rajhamundry
  4. Kakinada
   Kakinada

Question 411 :
The two northeastern hills that block the South West Monsoon winds and cause heavy rains in Assam are

  1. Arakan Yoma and Pegu Yoma
  2. Garo, Khasi and Jaintia hills
  3. Barail and Patkai hills
  4. Khasi and Pegu Yoma
   Garo, Khasi and Jaintia hills

Question 412 :

  1. rubber, oil seeds, groundnut

  3. tea, coffee, cocoa
