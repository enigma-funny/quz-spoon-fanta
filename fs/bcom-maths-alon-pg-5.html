Question 121 :
A 'less than' and the 'greater than' cumulative frequency curve helps to determine _____. (i) Mean, (ii) Median, (iii) Mode, (iv) Quartiles

  1. (i) - (ii)
  2. (ii) and (iii)
  3. (i) and (iv)
  4. (ii) and (iv)
  (ii) and (iv)
