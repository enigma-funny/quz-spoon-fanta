Question 1 :

Question 2 :
The collection transport processing recycling or disposal of waste material is called_______.

  1. Waste Management

  3. Plastic Management
  4. E-waste Management
   Waste Management

Which of the following is effective waste management?

  1. Biodegradable
  2. Disposal

Why it is difficult to recycle plastics?
