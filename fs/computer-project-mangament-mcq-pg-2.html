Question 51 :

Question 52 :
If an activity with free slack time of 2 weeks is delayed by 1 week

  1. the project will be delayed by 1 week.
  2. the slack time of all activities that follow this activity is reduced by 1 week.
  3. no other activity in the project is affected.
  4. the probability of completing the project on time decreases.

Question 53 :
You are in your third month of project execution which is expected to continue for a duration of 14 calendar months. Your sponsor has asked for a performance report. You decide to create a S curve. Based on the S curve you report your Actual Cost which is more than the Earned Value.Also the Planned Value is greater than the Earned Value. What would be the interpretation of this data ?

  1. The project is over budget and behind schedule
  2. The project is on schedule and on budget
  3. The project is over budget but on schedule
  4. The project is on budget but behind schedule
  The project is over budget and behind schedule
