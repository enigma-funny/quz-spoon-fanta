Question 31 :
Travelling to frangible and undisturbed landscapes on earth is a part of_______.

  1. Medical tourism
  2. Eco-tourism
  3. Religious tourism
  4. Coastal tourism

Question 32 :

  1. Religious tourism
  2. Medical tourism

Question 34 :
Why is recycled paper banned for use in food containers?

  1. Because it requires a lot of space
  2. Because it creates contamination
  3. Because paper can be used only one time
